Characteristic peculiarities of COffea

Morbid excitation of the organs of sense and of the nervous system, with sensitiveness to pain. 

Aggravation of the pains in the open air. 

Sad, weeping mood in the open air, with indisposition to work. 

Great nervousness, with lachrymation and weariness during a walk in the 
open air.  

Weariness of the limbs up to the thighs, after a walk in the open air.
