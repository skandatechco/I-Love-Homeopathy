ACUTE RESCUE :

A boy of 10 yrs presented with pain abdomen off and on for the past 2 months or so, has already been to a homoeopathic physician, who had also prescribed some mother tinctures for him, but there is no relief in the abdominal pain,pain is diffusely spread over the whole abdomen, pain assumes sharp intensity with doubling up, just for few moments, and it eases up for a while after passing flatus, he does not drink much water, stools are semisolid and 2-3 times a day, not hungry, eats due to parents insisting. With which remedy can we rescue this lad?
