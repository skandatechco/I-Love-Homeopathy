#### Can you identify the remedy that cured this lady?

##### A lady who had borne several children was subject to severe attacks of vertigo, which were attended with dimness of vision and fever, during which she would appear like one intoxicated.

##### The 3rd dilution, given night and morning in drop doses in water, effected a radical cure. I have also found **Remedy X**, an excellent remedy in low fevers when the pulse was slow, and particularly when by lifting or turning the patient the pulse would become accelerated.

This is a cured case from the clinic of Dr A. E. Small.
