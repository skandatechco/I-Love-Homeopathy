## CHARACTERISTIC PECULIARITIES OF CALADIUM SEGUINUM

#### Sleep in the day-time removes all his pains.

#### Many of the pains are relieved by the sweat in the first days of the proving.

#### He continues to sweat readily and a good deal, a long time after the proving.

#### Most of the symptoms occur in the day-time and during the hot stage ; in the evening he feels better again.
