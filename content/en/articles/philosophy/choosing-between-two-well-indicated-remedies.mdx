Occasionally two or more remedies may seem equally well indicated, and here comes a real perplexity for even the best prescribers.

A careful review of the symptoms, including conditions of aggravation and amelioration will generally clear up such perplexity.

All who have had real experience in this painstaking study know that the price of success is a strict compliance with the law of cure. This means a diligent and discriminating use of the Homeopathic Materia Medica and the treatment of the patient as a whole rather than treating the name of a disease.
