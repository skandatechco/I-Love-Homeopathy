WHAT WOULD YOU PRESCRIBE FOR.......NAOMI CAMPBELL?

"Anger is a manifestation of a deeper issue - and that, for me, is based on insecurity, self-esteem and loneliness...I'm always trying to do the impossible to please people. It comes from not being secure in myself and not looking at the things within I have to fix...I'm not set on a pedestal where I think I'm too high and mighty...I think what is very scary about cocaine is that you start to feel too confident and you start to feel indispensable. You become short-tempered, your little charm goes."
