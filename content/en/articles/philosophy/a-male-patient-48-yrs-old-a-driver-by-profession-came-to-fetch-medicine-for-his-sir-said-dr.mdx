ACUTE RESCUE : A male patient, 48 yrs old, a driver by profession, came to fetch medicine for his Sir, said- "Dr - Do u have some medicine for me too..?" Yes, pl detail me your symptoms. "Iam having a kind of lethargy, weakness, sadness (udaasi/sadma jaisa) for past 3-4 days, after I suffered from diarrhoea which is now controlled after antibiotics some doctor gave me, my appetite is okay, m thirst is okay, since morn till now (10 am)I have taken about 2 glasses of water, my stools are normal, but this weak feeling and I wish to just lie down in bed, but my profession is such that I have to be alert and active." Friends, please rescue this driver so that he is safe for himself and others , on the road. Aparna Singh ILH 15th May 2012
