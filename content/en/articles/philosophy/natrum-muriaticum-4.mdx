## Summary

Dr. Micheline Deltombe-Kopp, founder of the Association of French Speaking Homeopath Paediatricians, tells the story of an adopted child whose homeopathic medicine, *Natrum muriaticum* enabled him to live through the difficult hurdles of uprooting and integration. We publish as a posthumous tribute this second text which illustrates both her keen clinical eye and her wide homeopathic and paediatric knowledge.
