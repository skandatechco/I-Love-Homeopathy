PHILOSOPHY: CONSTANTINE HERING
Constantine Hering is widely known as "The Father of American Homeopathy"
and was profoundly revered by his contemporaries. His influence extended across the larger part of the USA for the best part of the 19th century with the result that homoeopathy flourished in that country for about 70 years. The motto he carried throughout his life was, " THE FORCE OF GENTLENESS IS GREAT"
