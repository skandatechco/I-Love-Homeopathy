When we go to an allopath and complain of pain, he only knows one kind of pain.
A pain that pains.
But when we go to a Homeopath he recognises that there are different kinds of Pain and each pain has a specific remedy.
For example, here is a list of the types of Toothache that you will have to help your homeopath with.
> BEATING OR PULSATING.
> BLUNT, OR WITHOUT FEELING.
> BORING.
> BRUISED OR ULCERATED, AS IF.
> BURNING.
> DIGGING.
> DRAWING, TEARING.
> GNAWING, SCRAPING, SCRATCHING ON THE NERVES.
> JERKING, TWITCHING.
> LONG, AS IF TOO.
> LOOSE.
> LOOSE, SENSATION AS IF.
> PIERCING.
> PRESSING.
> PRESSING, INWARDS.
> PRESSING, OUTWARDS.
> PRESSING, ASUNDER.
> PRESSING, AS IF FROM CONGESTION OF BLOOD, AS IF THE TEETH WERE TOO CLOSE.
> PULLED, OR LIFTED OUT, OR WRENCHED, AS IF.

Good Health with Homeopathy!
