PONDERINGS: The aim of medicine is to make for comfort and increase the span of life. Hahnemann contributed notably to this end when he brought into the light nature's own way of healing, from within. In doing so he evolved a new and unique method of dialing with symptoms and at the same time showed that success with the abnormal depends upon fully realizing what is normal.
