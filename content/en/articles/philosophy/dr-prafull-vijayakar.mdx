PHILOSOPHY: Dr. Prafull Vijayakar
in his Theory of suppression book, invites us to remember the
THE SEVEN CARDINAL PRINCIPLES from our Master Hahnemann
The seven cardinal principles in which the entire system
of medicine and homeopatic practice is based are:
1. The law of simple: that the medicines or remedies should be
prepared only with simple substances.
2. The law of Unique (one): only one remedy should be prescribed
at the time.
3. The law of similimum: the law of homeopathy "Similia Similibus
Curantur"
4. The law of the minimum: only the minimum quantity should be
prescribed and used to get a soft, rapid and permanent
cure.(or law of the minimum dose.)
5. Law of the dinamized drug: only dinamized remedies and potentized
should be used to cure.
6. The law of experimented drugs: only remedies that have been
proved on a healthy person should be used in our practice.
7. Theory of the Vital force: The vital force controls totaly the
function of the live organism and it is the autocratic supreme vital
force that governs with ilimitated power. In health state is the
responsable of the armonic function of the human body and
in sickness is only the modified state of the vital force.
From the book Predictive Homeopathy, Part 1 Theory of Supression.
