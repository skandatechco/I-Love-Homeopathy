ACUTE RESCUE : A child aged 8 yrs, mother of whom called me, saying.. Dr my child slept again in the forenoon, after playing for sometime in the morning, since it is his holiday today, I feel he is not well, he is dull and when well never sleeps at this time. I feel he may be having some kind of stomach upset too, since he's suffering from flatulence too, I had given him a dose of Ars album 30, from my box of medicines ,seeing that he was kind of restless too, though not thirsty.. I asked her how the child was after he woke up. she said he looks kind of okay but his one cheek is red and hot and the other is absolutely cold, and he seems to be a little dull too, is not having food or drink as he used to when well... Now let's rescue this child . Got any remedy in mind? Aparna Singh ILH 31st Jan 2012
