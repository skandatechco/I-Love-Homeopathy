Characteristic peculiarities of Anacardium

- Any part which he leaves unmoved, immediately goes to sleep).
- The attacks cease for one or two days, and then continue again for a couple of days, so that a certain periodicity may be observed inthe appearance of these symptoms.
- When sitting, he feels well; but standing causes an uneasiness in the lower extremities, as if they ought to be drawn up, with anxiety.
- While sitting quietly he perceives the beating of the pulse in the arms which had been loosely laid upon one another, nay, in the whole body (after some bodily effort).
