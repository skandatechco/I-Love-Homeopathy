Among the many absurd objections which Hahnemann had to meet and which his followers still have to meet is that Homœopathy "only treats symptoms and if it gets rid of them it leaves the disease untouched." The very opposite is the truth.
> **Homœopathy is compelled to extinguish the internal, morbid action before it can cause the external symptoms to vanish.**

The Allopathic method is to get rid of the symptoms even at the cost off producing other and worse symptoms. The outward and visible signs and symptoms are the guides to the internal and invisible causes -that is all.
