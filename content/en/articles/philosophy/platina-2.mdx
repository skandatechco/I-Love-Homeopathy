Characteristic peculiarities of PLATINA
***The symptoms of the body disappear as the symptoms of the mind appear, and vice versa.
Many symptoms are worse during rest, and relieved by motion.
Many symptoms are worse in the evening.
The symptoms which abate in the open air, are worse in the room.***
