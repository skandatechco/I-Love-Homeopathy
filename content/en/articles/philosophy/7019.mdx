PHILOSOPHY: SAMUEL HAHNEMANN Organon #23
All pure experience, however, and all accurate research
convince us that persistent symptoms of disease are far
from being removed and annihilated by opposite
symptoms of medicines (as in the antipathic, enantiopathic
or palliative method), that, on the contrary, after transient,
apparent alleviation, they break forth again, only with
increased intensity and become manifestly aggravated
(see #58-62 and 69)
