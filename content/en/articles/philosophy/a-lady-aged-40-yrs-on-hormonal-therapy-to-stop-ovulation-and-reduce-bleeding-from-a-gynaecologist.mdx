			2. I feel Iam now going to die, coz I cannot eat I cannot walk, Iam so weak.(mein marti hun abhi-in Hindi, she said)
			3.My right knee pains so much that daily I have to pop in a pill or two of Brufen (analgesic which is known to cause gastric/duodenal ulcerations).I can't walk without this pill.
			4. I f I stop these allopathic hormonal pills, there is flooding/heavy bleeding in a day or two which may last for 10-20 days, hence I cannot stop those.
			Now how to rescue her?
