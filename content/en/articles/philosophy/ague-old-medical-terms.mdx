Ague is the old medical term for conditions that are used to define the recurring fever & chills of malarial infection.

Old medical terms explained. Homeopathic texts are filled with old medical terms that could baffle fans and people who have little or no medical background.
Some of the terms are still used. The meanings are meant to give you a clue and understand the condition and the action of the remedy better.
