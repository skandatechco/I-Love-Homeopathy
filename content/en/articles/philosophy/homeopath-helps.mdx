PHILOSOPHY:

"How easy it is to incorporate in one's memory a certain number of prescriptions which one need only to recall to mind at the bedside of the patient in order to jot down one thing or other on a slip of paper. This requires scarcely two minutes.

In this way a dozen patients are prescribed for and got rid of in an incredibly short space of time.

But a Homeopathic physician must take the trouble to inquire about all the circumstances or conditions of the patient in order to be able to select a suitable remedy;in this time the ordinary physician can prescribe for three times as many patients. Yet the Homeopath helps the patient in a shorter time than does the Allopath."

~ Dr. Hahnemann
