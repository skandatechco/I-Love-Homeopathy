Characteristic peculiarities of Asafoetida

Intermittent, pulsative, or oppressive, darting, also tearing pains from within outwards, transformed into different kinds of pain.

Relieved by touching the parts, and sometimes accompanied with a feeling of numbness.

Pains in the planes of the flexor muscles.

The pains come on when sitting ; they abate during a walk in the open air.
