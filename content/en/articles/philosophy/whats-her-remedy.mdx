She believes herself married and pregnant, and is tormented by remorse for imagined crimes ; seeks constantly to escape and drown herself. Terrible anxiety from rush of blood to the head and heart. Is only quiet when lying undisturbed and brooding over her troubles which she rehearses in a doleful tone. If disturbed, she screams, strikes and tears things, crying all the while " I am neglecting my duty, breaking my oath !" Her face is pale and distorted.

What's her remedy?
