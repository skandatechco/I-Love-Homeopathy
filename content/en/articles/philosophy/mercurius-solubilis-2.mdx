Characteristic peculiarities of MERCURIUS SOLUBILIS
*The symptoms generally become worse in the evening and at night. The pains become intolerable in bed (at night).
The evening air is unpleasant to him. Chilliness when walking in the open air.
Palpitation of the heart when walking.
Sweat breaks out on the forehead, as soon as he walks in the open air. Violent sweat when walking. Sweat at every
motion. Sweat when drinking anything warm.
The ailments appear more frequently on the left side of the body. He feels better when walking, than when lying or sitting.*
