PHILOSOPHY: SAMUEL HAHNEMANN
"No walk of life is, according to the testimony of
all ages, more unanimously declared a conjectural art,
than medicine. None, therefore, has less right to
refuse a searching inquiry than this art, on which
rests the highest good in life, human health"
Samuel Hahnemann.
