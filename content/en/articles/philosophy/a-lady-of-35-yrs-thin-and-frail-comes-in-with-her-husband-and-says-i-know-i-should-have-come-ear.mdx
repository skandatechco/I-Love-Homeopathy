ACUTE RESCUE : A lady of 35 yrs, thin and frail comes in with her husband and says- " I know I should have come earlier, but this is an emergency. " Okay, tell me what is the emergency. See, I feel so weak and faint, and have a pressing sensation/pain in the back of my head, Iam also feeling giddy, I feel my blood pressure is low, as if I may just fall down, I work so much in the kitchen, and intend to finish up everything, but then I feel it becomes too much, I have my ailing mother in law to look after and my child and husband too. while she said this she sat stooped and and an anxious and agonised expression. Okay let me have a look at your tongue, it was pale and clean. I feel so weak and run down and giddy too. With which remedy, friends, can we rescue this woman? Aparna Singh ILH 3rd April 2012
