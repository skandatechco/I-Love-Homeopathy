PHILOSOPHY: Samuel Hahnemann,
His life and work Vol I Chapter XXVII
" Only those unbiased individuals to whom truth
and the happiness of mankind are of some value,
will be receptive to our simple doctrine of healing,
they alone will of┬átheir own free bring the necessary
sacrifice to draw forth to the light those unfathomable
treasures of medicinal effects which from the beginning
of time have been hidden in the night of ignorance,
self-sufficiency and ill-will.
This willingness I have rejoiced to find in my true pupils...
and this what is good will grow-but only on good soil"
Samuel Hahnemann
