### PHILOSOPHY:

Experience has shown me that it is most useful in diseases of any magnitude to give the patient the powerful homeopathic pellet or pellets only in solution, and this solution in divided doses.
In this way we give the medicine, dissolved in seven to twenty tablespoons of water without any addition, in acute and very acute diseases every six, four or two hours; where the danger is urgent, even every hour or half-an-hour, a tablespoon at a time
In chronic diseases, I have found it best to give a dose (e.g., a spoonful) of a solution of the suitable medicine at least every two days, more usually every day."
~ Dr. Hahnemann - Chronic Diseases.
