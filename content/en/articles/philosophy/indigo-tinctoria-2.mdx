Characteristic Peculiarities of Indigo Tinctoria

The pains are characterized by great intensity, are worst during rest and while sitting, and can frequently either be entirely suppressed by rubbing and. pressure or by motion, or at any rate, can be alleviated so as to re-appear with less intensity. 
— The majority of the pains come on or are aggravated in the afternoon and evening.
