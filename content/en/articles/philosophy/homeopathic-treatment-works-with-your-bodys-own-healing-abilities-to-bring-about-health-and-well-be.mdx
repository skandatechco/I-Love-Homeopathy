## The Philosophy of Homeopathy

What makes Homeopathy a better alternative rather than conventional remedies.
Homoeopathic treatment works with your body's own healing abilities to bring about health and well-being.
Homoeopathy treats the patients as individuals, not as a collection of disease labels. Homoeopathy treats symptoms collectively (ie. holistic) - mental, emotional and physical. Homoeopathic remedies are administered as minute amounts of natural substances.
