PHILOSOPHY: Samuel Hahneman
His life and work Vol I Chapter XII (Haehl R.)
"With heartfelt gratitude I acknowledge the infinite
mercy with which the Supreme Giver of all good things
has hitherto upheld my strength and cheerful mind
amidst all attacks of hostile men. Here, on earth,
I have no other desire than to be able to show the
world in a worthy manner those good principles for
the relieving of man's suffering, which the All-highest
allowed me to discover-I may almost say, which He
revealed to me. After that I shall die happily."
Samuel Hahnemann,
