PHILOSOPHY: HOMEOPATHIC PHILOSOPHY
Selecting the potency:
The level of health of the patient determines the potency to be
selected. In general we can say, a person in good health level,
we can give a high potency to start, this means that the more
compromised the defence mechanism, or total health level to
stimulate the inmune system the lower the
potency to start with. Patients with extreme sensibility to remedies,
patients sensible to mental and emotional impressions who have
symptoms do to a small stimulation, similar to what we see in the
nervous constitutions or people that suffers alergies are sensible to high
potency homeopathic remedies.
