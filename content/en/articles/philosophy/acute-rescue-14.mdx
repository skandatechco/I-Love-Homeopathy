Dr ,Since you gave me that medicine for pain abdomen , which was in the mid abdomen and a feeling of vomiting, which occurred after rich fatty food intake, I am feeling much better, but see I am so much troubled by this constant pain here, this is how she pointed it out. You know, she further said- "I am having this pain in the abdomen , indigestion also occurs more frequently nowadays. Now please relieve me of this too..." 
Any investigations needed? She is a lady of 60 years age, slim, fair, and too lethargic and dull, now eats only boiled food, vegetarian by habit. Now try to rescue her, can you?
