[!](http://ilovehomoeopathy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/hahneman8881.jpg)Hahnemann Lesser Writings, the Friend of Health.
I readily grant that excessive concern about one's health
is an evil, that there is no occasion for an active lad to
trouble himself about fur-boots, for a rosy-cheeked lass
to interest herself in the various kinds of obstetric forceps, or
for the pleasure seeker to concern himself about hospitals,
but everything has its proper bounds, every human being
his particular sphere, which he out to be thoroughly conversant
with, and which he should blush to be unacquainted with.
