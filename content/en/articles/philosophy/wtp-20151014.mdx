During the proving of this remedy, I caused the following symptoms
1. He woke up with great anxiety and solicitude, without cause, at 1 A.M.; unable to sleep; felt wide awake, with so many thoughts running through his head; on attempting to read, could only stare at the book on account of anxious thoughts running through his mind; about 3 o'clock he rose and dressed, and attempted to write, but was so absent-minded that he could not find words to express myself; the next day he was unable to perform any mental labor, not on account of disinclination to work, but from absence of thought.

2. He developed a fever, with a coldness running over the back, with constant inclination to yawn and desire to stretch out the feet.

Who am I?
