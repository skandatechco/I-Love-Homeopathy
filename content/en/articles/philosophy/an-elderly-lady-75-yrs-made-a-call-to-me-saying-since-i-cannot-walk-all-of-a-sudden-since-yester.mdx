ACUTE RESCUE : An elderly lady 75 yrs, made a call to me saying- "since I cannot walk, all of a sudden since yesterday, will you be kind enough to visit my place". when I saw her at her home, she was seated in a chair in the sun, and was knitting. After the courteous greetings, she said, see I can sit comfortably, but when I walk Iam limping since yesterday.
			I-How did it start?
			She-All of a sudden, when I woke up in the morning, I couldn't walk.
			I-Okay, and when is the pain more or less.
			She- Pain is in the right side of lower back, and radiating slightly to back of right thigh, Iam better when I wake up in morning, but then after little moving about it gets worsened, and I also cannot change sides at night..better by hot fomentation temporarily.
			Now friends, can u decide with which remedy can this elderly lady be rescued ?
