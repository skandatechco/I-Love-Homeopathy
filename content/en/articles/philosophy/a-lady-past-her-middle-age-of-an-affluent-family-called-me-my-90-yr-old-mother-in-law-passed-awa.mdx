ACUTE RESCUE : A lady past her middle age, of an affluent family called me, -"my 90 yr old mother in law passed away a week ago, and you can't imagine 25-30 people coming to my house everyday and arranging for all their needs ,food and everything, and now Iam so exhausted mentally, physically emotionally," and as she said this , I could feel from the tone of her voice how really exhausted she was, " I don't want to see anybody , talk to anybody, I feel like running away from all this and have been crying after I used to get overworked, and a month before she passed away I looked after her so well in the hospital, besides the treatment the doctors were giving her for her dehydration and typhoid, I was reciting the bhajans and Bhagvadgita to her, and since that time the exhaustion had begun gradually, but now it's beyond my efforts , and I keep sweating off and on."
		How can we rescue this lady with our wonderful miracle medicines in potencies ? Aparna Singh ILH 03 July 2012
