ACUTE RESCUE : This young lad of 18 blossoms presented like this as in the snap. This has been there for the past 3 days, gradually getting this big. Pl discard the purple hue, since that is due to gentian violet which he had applied before coming to me. The furuncle is a bit hard and tender all around. He limps when walking due to pain in the leg. Now rescue him with an appropriate similimum. Aparna Singh ILH 5th Feb 2013
