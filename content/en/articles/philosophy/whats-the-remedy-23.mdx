***Timid Tom cannot be calm
Imagines dead people , has fear of evil.
Cannot seems to remember
Feels like in slumber,
Wants to be in motion,
But feels worse with this notion.
Catches cold in a weather so wet,
Open air he loves the best ;
Throat is dry with a thirst so extreme,
Its swollen inflammed with heartburn in between.
Tired Tom has amorous dreams so clear,
Remedy's done ,lest he may suffer from fear !***
