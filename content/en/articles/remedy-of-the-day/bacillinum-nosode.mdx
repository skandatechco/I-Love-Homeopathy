REMEDY OF THE DAY: BACILLINUM-Nosode
Tincture made either from the sputum or maceration of rubercular lung.
This nosode is named and first described by Dr. Burnett, a famous
Homeopath. Bacillinum is used for weak lungs of elderly people and for
children with chronic catarrhal and attacks of suffocation at night with
difficult cough and chronic catarrhal conditions.
Use one dose, not under 30C, is quick in action, no repetition.
