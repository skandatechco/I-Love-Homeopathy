REMEDY OF THE DAY : ARANEA DIADEMA (Papal-cross spider)
All spider poisons powerfully affects the nervous system.
Aranea symptoms are characterized by periodicity and coldness
and great susceptibility to dampness, It is the remedy for the
constitution favorable to malarial poisoning, where every damp
day or place favors chilliness. Patient feels cold to the very bones.
Coldness not relieved by anything.
They feel as if parts were enlarged and heavier, they wake up at
night with a feeling their hands are twice their natural size.
Spleen swollen, Abnormal sensitiveness to damp cold, that is why
is for hydrogenoid constitution. inability to live near fresh water,
lakes rivers, etc., or in damp, chilly places.
Pain in trifacial nerve, sudden teeth pain from periphery to inwards.
confusion better by smoking in open air.
Menses too early and copious.
Cronic intermittent fever sunny days.
< periodicity, same hour, day month, etc.
