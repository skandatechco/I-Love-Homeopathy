REMEDY OF THE DAY: TARAXATUM OFFICINALE
Allen JC, Materia Medica,
Generalities: Inner feeling as if very sick, all limbs
painful on touch or when in an uncomfortable position
Restlessness and disconfort, weakness, so that
he wished to lie or sit, shen he was semi-conscious
as on falling asleep. Most symptoms appear and
disappear when walking, Talkative, inclined to laugh,
Religious, happy, contented, Ill humor in morning and
disinclined to any occupation.
Mapped tongue, covered with white film, sensation of
rawness in it, afterwards the firm comes out in pieces.
< 8pm, at night gastric illments, chilliness < after eating,
nose, hands and tips of fingers ICY COLD. Nocturnal
perspiration. Good for stimulating the liver,
Pressive pains in the last thre fingers of the right
hand. This remedy for bilious diarrhea, soreness
over the liver, bitter taste, mapped tongue
During fever great restlessness, with tearing pains in
the legs, intolerable while at rest constant muttering
delirium, violent tearing in the occiput.
