Wild snake gourd, parwal or patola, potol, in India called
green potato, a good source of carbohidrates, vit A and C
Used for constipation, fever, skin infections and wounds and
alopecia areata.
Chronic malaria fever, kala-azaar, nausea and vomiting
Despondent, vertigo while lying in bed, headache during fever
empty feeling of abdomen and hot feeling at 11 am owing
to enlargement of the liver and spleen. pain < sneezing, coughing
and motion.
Copious stool-greenish yellow-liquid mixed with bile and mucus.
Bloody stool becomes exhausted after evacuation.
Fever comes at 11am to 12 noon with chilliness during fever
burning sensations of the whole body. Headache with thirst.
fever with bilious symptoms, Urine red, retention during vomit.
