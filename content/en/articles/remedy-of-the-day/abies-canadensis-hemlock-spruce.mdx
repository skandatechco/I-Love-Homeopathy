REMEDY OF THE DAY: ABIES CANADENSIS, Hemlock Spruce,
Allen T.F. Encyclopedia of Pure Materia Medica.
Tincture made of the bark and young buds.
Mind: Quiet, careless, but easily fretted, Irritable.
Head a tipsy feeling, a swimming of the head, he
feels lightheaded. Eyes feeling like that produced
by a stye in the outer canthus of the left eye.
Dryness of the mouth, Hungry. a tendency to eat far
beyond the capacity for digestion. Rumbling of stomach
and bowels after eating. Caving for meat, pickles,
and other coarse food, belching. distension of the
epigastrium, with increased action of the heart,
Gnawing, hungry faint feeling at the epigastrium.
Irritable feeling in the splenic region.
Rectum burns, constipation, Urinates frequently day
and night, urine straw colored.
shrunken cold hands, Twitching of muscles, Generalities:
bloated, wants to lie down all the time,
excessively weak, very faint, as if the top of the
head were congested, drunken feeling, skin clammy.
Fever, cold shivering all over, as if the blood turned
to ice water. chills down the back.
Beatriz H Hill
