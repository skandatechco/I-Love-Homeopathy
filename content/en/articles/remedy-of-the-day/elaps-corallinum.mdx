## REMEDY OF THE DAY: ELAPS CORALLINUM

Black discharges cerumen, blood, etc., cold things disagree,
FEARS RAIN, apoplexy
Depressed, imagines someone is talking, dreads to be alone.
can speak but cannot understand, chest constriction, absent
minded, spirit depression, desire to be alone, but bears to
be alone as if something bad will happen, mad with himself,
and does not like to be talked. Sensation as if all blood was
in the head, Noises in ears as if lightning inside.
sweetened buttermilk desires. Nausea and vomiting. Prostrating
diarrhea of consumption. Acidity of the stomach, with faint feeling.
Sudden pain in the stomach. Spasm of easophagus; pharynx constricted;
food and liquids suddenly arrested, and then fall heavily into stomach.
Spasms followed by paresis. Cold feeling in stomach.
Fruits and ice-water lie very cold. Right-sided paralysis.
Must have oscillatory motion. Rheumatic constitutions.
Ear, nose and throat symptoms important.
Feels as if the pleura is being pulled.
Head.--Violent headache, extending from forehead to occiput;
first one eye, then the other. Pain in ears. Vertigo with a tendency
to fall forward. Weight and pain in forehead. Fullness in the head.
Eyes.--Aversion to light; letters run together when reading.
Veil before eyes. Burning in lids. Bloated around the eyes in
the morning. Large red fiery spot before eyes.
WORSE: eating fruits, cold drinks, wet weather.
tips of fingers peel off, itching eruptions in axillae
Ice cold feet, vesicular eruptions on feet, arms and hands.
knee joints feel sprained.
BOERICKE M. M.
Beatriz H Hill, Medicina Homeopatica Behhill
