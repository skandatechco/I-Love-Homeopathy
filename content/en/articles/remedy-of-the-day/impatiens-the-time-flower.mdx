**Impatiens- The time flower** (impatiens glandulifera)
From Impatience to patience
*“Those who are quick in thought and action who wish all things to be done without hesitation or delay. When ill they are anxious for a hasty recovery. They find it difficult to be patient with people who are slow, as they consider it wrong and a waste of time.”– Edward Bach*
**Positive qualities:**

- Patience
- Acceptance
- Flowing with the pace of life and others

**Common patterns of imbalance:**

- Impatience
- Irritation
- Tension
- Intolerance

**Homeopathics that compliment:**

- Arsenicum Album
- Nux Vomica
