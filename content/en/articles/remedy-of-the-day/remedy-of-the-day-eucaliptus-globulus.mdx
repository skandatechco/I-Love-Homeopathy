REMEDY OF THE DAY: EUCALIPTUS GLOBULUS
Blue gum tree- native of australia- grown all over the world
The floral emblem of Tasmania
Tincture of fresh leaves
A powerful anticeptic and destructive to low forms of life, a stimulating
expectorant and an efficient diaphoretic.
Atonic dispepsia, gastric and intestinal catarrah. This is a special
remedy with marked effects on catarrhal processes, malaria treatment
and intestinal disturbances. Influenza. Fevers of relapsing character
produces diuresis and great increase of urea.
Hemorrhages internally or locally
useful in typhoid fever, symptoms of exhustion or toxemia.
