The chief remedy in this disease; white- coated tongue; breasts swollen and painful; to prevent ''caking." 
Alternate with Ferrum phos. 

If a few doses of Ferrum phos. and Kali mur. are given about the time the secretion of milk is expected, milk fever will be avoided.
