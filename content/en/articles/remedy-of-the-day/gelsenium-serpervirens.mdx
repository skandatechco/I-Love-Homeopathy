IN INFLUENZA Materia Med. Bahtia,
Dullness, dowsiness, dizziness, dim sight, deliriium
characterise Gels. patient. Sensitive to falling barometer,
cold and dampness bring on complaints. No thirst.
Chills in back. Chills and heat chase one another.
Heaviness and tiredness of body and limbs. Headache
is most in the nape of the neack upwards to occiput
settling in the eyes. Coryza with violent sneezing.
< morning, Ritht nostril stopped. discharge waterly
and excoriating. Sore throat < left nostril, hard painful
cough, swallowing causes shooting in the ears. Hands
may tremble, pain moving eyes, slow pulse, Urine
is copious which ameliorates. Ameliorates from sweat
and stimulants. Fainting if anything annoying him to
anger. feels heart is involve, and remember that strangely
enough people thinks unless he is on the constant move
his heart will stop beating.
