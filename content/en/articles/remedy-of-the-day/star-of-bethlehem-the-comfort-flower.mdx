(Ornithogalum Umbellatum-white)
*The Comfort Flower*
From Shock to Reorientation
“For those in great distress under conditions which for a time produce great unhappiness*. The shock of serious news, the loss of someone dear, the fright following an accident and such like”* – Edward Bach
 
**Positive Qualities:**

- Soothing
- Healing
- Inner Divinity

**Patterns of Imbalance:**

- Shock
- Trauma (recent or past)
- Need for comfort or assurance

- Aconite
- Arnica
- Gelsemium
- Ignatia
