REMEDY OF THE DAY: TARENTULA CUBENSIS (cuban spider)
Dr. Margaret Lucy Tyler once wrote:
OH! this hairy spider it is one of the most efficacious remedies for
boils, abcesses, felons or swelling of any kind where the tissues put
on a bluish color, and there are intense burning pains.
Diphteria, and various forms of malignant supuration. The remedy
is also good for Pains of death, soothes the last struggles. \
Has restless feet. Intermittent septic chills. Bubonic plage as
preventive remedy especially during periods of invasion.
Better smoking worse at night where ameliorates walking all night.
Has loss of appetite except for breakfast.
Also good for Grangre, genital pruritus < during night.
It can be used when great postration and diarrhea with inttermittent
fever of evening exacerbation, relieving the atrocious pains
accompanying this condition, it acts almost like magic,
has extreme sensibility to contact and touch of the affected parts.
and do not forget the spiders basic symptoms, anxiety, restlesness
vengative, violent, loves or hates music, etc. etc.
