**Vine** (Vitis Vinifera)
*The Authority Flower*
"From Leading to being Led"
*"Very Capable people, certain of their own ability,confident of success. Being so assured they think that it would be for the benefit of others if they could be persuaded to do things as they themselves do. " - Edward Bach*
**Positive Qualities:**

- Selfless service
- Tolerance for others to themselves
- Respectful of others

**Patterns of Imbalance:**

- Fanatical
- Domineering
- Tyrannical
- Forceful of will upon others
