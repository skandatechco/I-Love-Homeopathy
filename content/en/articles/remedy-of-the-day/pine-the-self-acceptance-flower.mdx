**Pine** (Pinus Sylvestris)
*The Self-Acceptance Flower*
From Self-Negation to Self-Respect
“*For those who blame themselves. Even when successful, they think they could have done better, and are never content with their efforts or the results They are hardworking and suffer much from the faults they place on themselves.”* – Edward Bach
 
**Positive Qualities:**

- Self- Acceptance
- Self- Forgiveness
- Freedom from inappropriate guilt
- Freedom from inappropriate blame

**Patterns of Imbalance:**

- Guilt
- Self-Blame
- Self-Criticism
- Inability to accept oneself

**Homeopathics Related**

- Ignatia
- Sepia
