REMEDY OF THE DAY: NUX MOSCHATA
Nutmeg, adapted to women and children of a nervous
hysterical temperament, people with dry skin who rarely
perspire, complaints of pregnancy.
Weakness of old age, dyspepsia of old people
Oversensitive to light, hearing, smell, to touch
Its ailments accompanied by DROWSINESS and SLEEPINESS
or an inclination to faint, even from slight pain, and its
complaints cause sleepiness.
Stupor and insensibility, absence of mind, cannot think,
great indifference to every-thing, weakness or loos of
memory, vanishing of thoughts while reading, talking or
writing, uses wrong words, does not recognize well-known
streets, changes humor from laughing or crying, from
"sudden change from grave to gay, from lively to serene"
Dryness of eye, too dry to close eyelids, DRY MOUTH,
tongue so dry it adheres to roof of mouth, saliva like cotton,
throat dry stiffened, no thirst.
Enormous distended abdomen after meals, eating too much
causes headache, Diarrhea in summer, from cold drinks
epidemic in autumn, white stools, from boiled milk during
dentition, during pregnancy with sleepiness and fainting,
At every menstrual nisus, mouth, throat and tongue become
intolerably dry, worse sleeping. Leucorrhea in place of menses
Pain, nausea and vomiting during pregnancy, from wearing
pessaries, sudden hoarseness from walking against dry wind,
Cough caused by getting warm in bed, over-heated, during
pregnancy, bathing, standing in water, living in cold damp
places, loose after eating, dry after drinking.
Sleep, irresistibly drowsy, sleepy, muddled, as if intoxicated,
coma lies silent, immovable, eyes constantly closed,
Rhreuma from getting feet wet, exposure to drafts of air
while heated, worse in cold, wet weather or cold wet
clothes, of left shoulder.
Backache while riding in a carriage, fatigue after exertion.
Nux m, antidotes MERCURIAL INHALATION, LEAD COLIC,
Oil of turpentine, spirituous liquors, and especially the
effects of bad beers. Agg. cold, wet windy weather,
weather changes, cold food, water and cold washing,
carriage driving, lying on painful side. Amel: in dry, warm
weather, warm room, wrapping up warmly.
