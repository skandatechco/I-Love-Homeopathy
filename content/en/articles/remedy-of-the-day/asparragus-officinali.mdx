REMEDY OF THE DAY: ASPARRAGUS OFFICINALI
Preparation, tincture of the young sprouts.
Mind-Serenity of mind the whole day. A peculiar anxiety, with
palpitations of the heart and ill-humor.
Severe coriza and naal catarrh, with profuse secretion of thin
whitish from left nostril afterwards from the right nostril, with
dryness and slight swelling of the internal nostril followed by
impeded entrance of air- pale face.
Allen Timothy, Encyclopedia of Pure Materia Medica.
Sexual excitement very marked, urine very offensive, sticking pains
in the urethra or sides of the penis, fine stinging in the meatus, with
a sensation after urination as if something was still passing; lithic or
greasy deposits on sides of vessel. Frequent but scanthy discharge
with a sensation as if something was sticking in the urethra, swelling
of the penis, with erections and urging to urinate, urine of a stinking
odor, cutting in the urethra (Book Volume 2 therapeutics of gonorrhoea.)
Dr. Allen J.H. The chronic Miasms, Sycosis, Psora and Pseudo -psora.
