**Red Chestnut** (Aesculus Carnea-red)
*The Cutting Free Flower*
From Symbiosis to Autonomy
“For those who find it difficult not to be anxious for other people. Often they have ceased to worry about themselves, but for those whom they are fond of they may suffer much, frequently anticipating what some unfortunate thing may happen to them*.”* – Edward Bach
 
**Positive Qualities:**

- Caring for others with calm inner peace
- Trust in the unfolding of life and life events

**Patterns of Imbalance:**

- Obsessive fear for others
- Worry for others
- Fearful for others
- Anticipating problems for others

- Arsenicum Album
