REMEDY OF THE DAY: LUFFA AMARA (Titpolla)
Also called Dhamarjab, first prover was Dr. S.C.Ghosh
Good for treatments of enlarged spleen, fever, hepatic congestion.
and also used as a tonic.
Very anxious persons, with dry mouth with excessive thirst and
...bitter taste.
Vomiting is watery or bile, passes loose stool rice watery at every fifteen
minutes intervals, sometimes mucus appeared every half an hour.
sometimes vomiting and purging simultaneously.
Burning sensation all over the body, sometimes with chilliness
Feeble, wek puls, body cold with perspiration.
(Banerjea SK, Fifty homeopathic Indian Drugs.)
