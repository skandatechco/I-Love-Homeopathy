REMEDY OF THE DAY: PENTHORUM SEDOIDES

Common name: Virginia Stonecrop

[!](http://ilovehomoeopathy.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/PenthorumSedoides0.jpg)A remedy for coryza, with rawness and wet feeling in nose. Throat feels raw. Chronic disorders of mucous membranes, with irritability. Chronic post-nasal catarrh; chronic pharyngitis, mucous membrane purple and relaxed. Posterior nares feel moist and raw; nose and ears feel full. Aphonia, hoarseness, relaxed vocal cords. Hypersecretion of mucous membranes. Itching of anus and burning in rectum. Trouble in pharyngeal vault and Eustachian tube.
