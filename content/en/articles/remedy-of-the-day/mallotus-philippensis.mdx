A tree from India, South Arabia, Tropical Austrialia
"A red inflamable powder, obtained from the trilobed
capsules of the plant" THE TAPEWORM REMEDY.

"Among the Arabs of Aden is also used to remove
freckles and pustules, it is also used to treat en
eruption named WILDFIRE"(erysilepa like rash)
strophulus volaticua, in children.

moist lint, but its real valme is to use it as
an anti helmintic when tapeworms are bothering body and
has been extensively used in INDIA.
