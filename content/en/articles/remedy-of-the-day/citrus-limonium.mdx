REMEDY OF THE DAY: CITRUS LIMONIUM
The remedy is made of the lemon juice.
scorbutus, sore throat, cnacer pain, excessive menstruation
calms the nervous system, frew drops of the tincture.
soothes constipation, convulsions, Diarrhea, Disenteria,
Disnea, Edemas, splenitis, hemorrage, Insolation Rheumatism.
Local applied to cancerous wounds one parte of remedy eight
of water to sooth cancer pain.
Rigidity of the joints, violent movements of arms and feet.
Fear disposition, slow opulse or fast., convulsive disposition
agravated by light.
MM. J.H.Clarke.

AURANTUM ORANGE, (Neuralgia and skin itching.)
