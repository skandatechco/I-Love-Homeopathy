HOMEOPATHY KIDS' TIPS
CONVULSIVE FEVER: OBSERVE THE CHILD....
ACONITUM - Great restlessness, thirst for cold water, worse
after exposure to cold weather, or cold, high fever, in children
who already talk, fear to die. Ailments from being scared.
...BELLADONA: Cold feet and cold hands, (convulsions because of
fever), rubor, violence without thirst. Screams while at sleep.
HYOSIAMUS. Stearing eyes, mental confusion, takes off
blanket, involuntary stool and urine.
STRAMONIUM: Delirium, talks incoherent, desire to escape from
the mothers arms, sees animals, the baby screams, hits, desires
light and company...
CALCAREA CARBONICA: typically soft, over-fat, chilly and
lethargic. they do not process mental or physical energy.
Chobby, slow for school, slow at games, liable to sprain their
ankles, weak muscles, sweat on exertion and constantly take
fresh colds. Big belied, enlarged tonsils, enlarged cervical
glands. They cannot bear to be laughed at. tend to diarrhea,
stools are pale, but are constipated and confortable with it.
Ailments from hearing scary stories. Car sickness, train-
sickness, dislike hot food. Like Ice-cream, aversion to meat.
craves eggs. Acute night mares, wakes up screaming
seeing horrible faces in the dark.
CALCAREA PHOS: hypertrophy of adenoid tissue, enlargement
of tonsils and cervical glands.. Brighter at school but with
headache if overworked. Muscular pains.
PHOSPHORUS. Afraid of dark, thunder, anxious, sensitive
dislikes for being alone, excitement after taking hot foods,
no desire for eggs, fond of meat and salty taste. night
terrors. Gets colds but not affecting throat, but lungs
because is sensitive to changes in temperature.
