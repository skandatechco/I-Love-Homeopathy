REMEDY OF THE DAY: ARBUTUS ANDRACHNE
The greek strawberry tree, is an evergreen shrub or small tree native of
mediterranean region. Arbutus=the strawberry tree Andrachne= the greek name. The fruit is redish-orange and used to make jelly, cadied fruit, alcohol beverages.
Tincture made of young shoots.
Arthritis, eczema, gout, rheumatism, urine disorders
This is Dr. Cooper's remedy and he cured excema with it, observed
that it produced pains in the big joints of a gouty or rheumatic character, so hence
he led to give in certain inveterate cases of arthritis.
Clears urine.
Arbutin= used for urine disorders by physicians.
