**Vervain-** (Verbena Officinalis)
*The Enthusiasm Flower*
"From World Savior to Light Bearer."
"Those with fixed principles and ideas, which they are confident are right, and rarely change which they very rarely change. They have a great wish to convert all around them to their own views of life. They are strong of will and have much courage when they are convinced of of those things that they wish to teach."
**Positive Qualities:**

- Ability to practice moderation
- Tolerance
- Balance
- Grounded Idealism

**Patterns of Imbalance:**

- Overbearing
- Intolerant Behavior
- Over-Enthusiasm
- Extreme Fanat
