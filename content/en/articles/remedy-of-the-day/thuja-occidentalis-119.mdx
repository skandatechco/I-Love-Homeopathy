REMEDY OF THE DAY: THUJA OCCIDENTALIS
ANSHUTZ, EP, Sexual Ills and Diseases Materia Medica
Very scupulous, fancies some one is along side, thinks
one's self double, or that an animal is in the abdomen,
sensation that the body is thin and delicate. That will
brake like glass. Worse in rainy weather. Vertigo on
closing the eyes. Useful for all fungous, warty growths,
fig-warts, polypi and cauli flower excrescences and
pear-shapped warts. Incontinence of urine in
warty persons. Gonorrhea when complicated with
condylomata. i.e., fleshy or warty growths, gonorrheal
rheumatism. Gleet when complicated with excrescences.
Elevated syphilitic excrescenses. Leucorrhoea of warty
women. Skin diseases, especially on the covered or
hidden parts. Theeth decay next to gums. Illness
from and following vaccination. In Dr. Proceso Sanchez
book, says that children who have parents that had
gonorrhea, can show all the symptoms of gonorrhea
even a yellow leucorrhea in girls without having the
disease. So thake this in consideration I had a case
lake that . Beatriz H Hill
