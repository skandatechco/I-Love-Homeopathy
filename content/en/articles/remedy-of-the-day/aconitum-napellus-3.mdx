The Homeopathic Therapeutics of Diarrhoea

FREQUENT... (dysenteric stool); Involuntary (when passing
flatus)
Aggravation: In summer, with hot days and cold nights: After being overheated: After exposure to cold, dry wind or a draught.
After anger or fright. After suppressed perspiration: at night,
after eating fruit, in infants.
Amelioration: After eating warm soup(pains)
Before stool: cutting pains, nausea and seat, anguish,
During stool: Cutting pains, Tenesmus, sweat, much flatus
with watery stool
After stool Relief, except from anguish, nausea and sweat,
In Cholera, bluish face, lips black, terror expression, cold limbs with blue nails, often able to cut short dysentery and even cholera morbus without any other remedy. well followed byDulcamara or belladonna, Abuse of Acon. calls for Sulph.
