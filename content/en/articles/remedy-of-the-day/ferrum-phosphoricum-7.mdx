### Schuessler's salts for Hoarseness.

Painful hoarseness of speakers and singers, with inflammation; from overexertion of the voice, or from taking cold; scraping of the throat, with sensation of dryness.
Cured cases
Mr. P. , a minister, was troubled with hoarseness after preaching; there was constant hemming and scraping of the throat. Calcarea phos. and [Ferrum phoshoricum](https://www.bahola.co/product/ferrum-phosphoricum/?v=c86ee0d9d7ed) , occasional small doses, continued for several months, entirely cured the affection.
