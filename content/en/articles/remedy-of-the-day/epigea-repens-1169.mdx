REMEDY OF THE DAY: EPIGEA REPENS
Gravel plant, mayflower, shadflower, gound laurel, mountain pink,
winter pink or Trailing arbutus, Massachusetts state flower.
for Urinary calculi, dysuria, gravel, strangury.
a Popular remedy in renal calculi throught the United States
ever since the settlement.
Will appear bloody purulent sediment in urine with intense dysuria,
and copious fine brown sand, is relieved by this remedy.
