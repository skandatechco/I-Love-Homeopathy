REMEDY OF THE DAY: IPECACUANHA,
Adapted to cases where the gastric symptoms predominate.
IN ALL DISEASES WITH CONSTANT AND CONTINUAL NAUSEA.
tincture made of dried root, Fluidextractum ipecacuana Brazil.
Hahnemann proved it, also for conjunctivitis, and inflammation
of the mucous membrane and the nose and bronchial tubes.
Its chief action is on the ramifications of the pneumo-gastric
nerve, producing spasmodic irritation in chest and stomach.
Malarious states hence its great use.
In headache? sensations as if the bones of the head
were crushed or bruised, there is an unilateral sick
headache over one eye. with deathly nausea and very
pale face, there is a drawn, nauseated expression about
the mouth.
Stomach: Intense nausea and vomiting, which are
followed by exhaustion and sleepiness, worse from fat
food, pork, pastry, candy, etc. stomach with a hanging
down relaxed feeling.
Ipeca stool, green, bloody or fermented, looking like molasses.
accompanied by griping about the umbilicus, worse by
motion.
Respiratory system, spasmodic cough, ending in choking
and gagging, and a tickling which extends from the larynx
to the extremities of the bronchi. Coarse rales all over the
chest, with violent paroxysms of coughing and retching
with pale face, great dyspnea, incessant cough with every
breath. Spasmodic asthma, with weight and anxiety in chest.
Whooping cough, convulsive cough, where the child stiffens
and becomes pale or blue and loses its breath, great
nausea and relief from vomiting.
