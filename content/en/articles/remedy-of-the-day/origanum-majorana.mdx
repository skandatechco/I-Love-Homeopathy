Not to be confused with Oregano
A treatise on the 'Sexual Passion" by the late Dr. Gallavardin, Lyons, France contains this item on ORIGANUM.
Acts on nervous system generally, and is effective in masturbation and excessively aroused sexual impulses. Affections of the breasts (Bufo). Desire for active exercise impelling her to run.
Frotomania; powerful lascivious impulses; leucorrheôa; hysteria. Lascivious ideas and dreams.
Compare: Ferula glauca (in violent sexual excitement in women; icy coldness in occiput); Plat; Valer; Canth; Hyos.
Reference: HOMEOPATHIC MATERIA MEDICA
by William BOERICKE ; FORGOTTEN REMEDIES.
