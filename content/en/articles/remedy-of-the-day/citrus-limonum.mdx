REMEDY OF THE DAY: CITRUS LIMONUM
Lemon juice: used for Cancer pains, constipation,
Diarrhea, dysenteria disnea, edematous states, escorbutic gums, splenitis, sun stroke, hemorrage, anemia
aplied locally (one part of lemon juice and 8 of water)
will relieve cancer pain. has a great effect on the blood.
increases the curative effect of belladona.
can be used as antidote for Stramonium giving tea spoons
of the juice to antidote stram. removing convulsive
attacks with violent movements of hands and feet.
convulsive eyes, fixed eyes, salivation, red face
Lose of mind, insanity and disposition to fear.
small rapid pulse. Convulsions are renovated by light.
Will also be useful in swollen painful joints, with a
sore pain like Ruta.
Can successfully be used for scorbutic states, cefalea
everyday. inflamation and splenomegalia with pain.
can faint, with small pulse, disnea, hemorrhage and edema.
