**Elm - The Responsibility Flower**(Ulmus Procera)
*"From Self Worth Crisis to Inner Confidence"*
"Those who are doing good work, are following the calling of their life and who hope to do something of importance, and this is often for the benefit of humanity. At times there may be periods of depression when they feel that the task they have undertaken is too difficult, and not within the power of a human being."-Edward Bach
**Positive qualities:**

- Joyous service
- Restore calm
- Be in your true calling
- Confidence
- Know your limits

**Common patterns of imbalance:**

- Exhaustion
- Depression
- Overwhelm
- Fatigue

**Homeopathics that compliment:**

- Nux Vomica
- Sepia
