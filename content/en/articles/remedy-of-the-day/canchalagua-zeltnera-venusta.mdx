REMEDY OF THE DAY: CANCHALAGUA - Zeltnera Venusta
Allen T.F., Hand Book of Mateia Medica and
Homeopathic Therapeutics.
Sore all over body, especially in lower limbs.
That kind of pain in head and fingers which accompanies
attacks of intermittent fever. Head feels congested,
Pain in forehead. Fulness, tightness of scalp, it feels
as if drawn together by India-rubber. Burning in left
eye, then in right. Stitches in ears, pain in ears.
Increased buzzing and roaring in ears.
Spitting of white mucus, with trembling and
nervousness. Appetite increased.
Regurgitations, waterbrash, ructus, flatus, pain in
abdomen, amel. pressure, loose stools, several a day.
Constipation, morning stool hard, knotty. Chills
repeatedly down spine and all over body. agg. in
bed, at night. Sleeplessness. Heat in whole body,
and after the proving could bear better the cool
trade wind.
Beatriz H Hill
