Also called Dhamarjab, first prover was Dr. S.C.Ghosh 			
Good for treatments of enlarged spleen, fever, hepatic congestion and also used as a tonic. Very anxious persons, with dry mouth with excessive thirst and bitter taste.
			
Vomiting is watery or bile, passes loose stool rice watery at every fifteen minutes intervals, sometimes mucus appeared every half an hour. Sometimes vomiting and purging simultaneously. Burning sensation all over the body, sometimes with chilliness.

Feeble, weak puls, body cold with perspiration.
