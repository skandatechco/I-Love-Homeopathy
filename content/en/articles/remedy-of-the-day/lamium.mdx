REMEDY OF THE DAY: LAMIUM
Lamium album, Dead nettle, white nittle, bee nettle.
Hemorrhoids, headache, hernia, leucorrhea, menstruation too
early.
Proved by Hahnemann, and others. Produces a Cephalea,
deep in headache with head moving back and foward as a
key note of the remedy.
Whining mood, inclination to weep, feels as abondoned, great
sadness, with lachymose humor, Great agitation and
anxiety, which permit no rest.
Head aches in center of brain < rising from a stooping
posture < rising from seat.
>when sead, this cephalalgia as if brain were compressed
on all sides, with very violent pains in centre of brain.
Excessive tightness of scalp, esp. near coronal suture.
Sight and hearing diminished.
Farrington recomends it for external hemorroids, and
hard stool with blood.
Urine tract, sensation as if a drop of water is passing
through urethra and draining. Tearing and drawing limbs
heel ulcers, .
