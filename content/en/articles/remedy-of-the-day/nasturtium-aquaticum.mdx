REMEDY OF THE DAY: NASTURTIUM AQUATICUM
Common name:
English ~ Shamrock; Stertion; Water-Cress
French ~ Cailli, Cresson.
German: Braukersch, Kersche
Medicinal part: Whole plant
Nasturcium is useful in scorburic affections, constipation, stricture of Urinary organs, aphrodisiac, antidote to tobacco, narcosis, sedative in neurotic affections, neurasthenia, hysteria, cirrhosis of liver, and dropsy.
