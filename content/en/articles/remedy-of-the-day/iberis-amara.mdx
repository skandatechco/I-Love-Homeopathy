REMEDY OF THE DAY: IBERIS AMARA
Bitter candy-tuft.
Preparation tincture of the ripe seeds.
Remember this remedy in HYPERTROPHY of the heart where there is
violent action which disturbs the patient, with a choking sensation and a
violent sharp action of the heart,
Pericarditis, myocarditis an nervous derangement of the heart with its
action is characterized by violence and irritability.
There are severe stitching pains in the cardiac region, the pulse is
full but irregular, and all the symptoms are aggravated by motion.
Disturbances of the heart following influenza, weakness of the heart's
action.
The heart's action developed a feeling of weakness with
irregular palpitation and great anxiety, is conscious of the heart's action
and is aggravated from tobacco and from wine.
