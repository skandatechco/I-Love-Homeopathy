**Oak** (Quercus Robur- Red)
*The Endurance Flower*
From Unflagging Duty to Inner Commitment
“*For those who are struggling and fighting strongly to get well, or in connection with the affairs of their daily life They will go on trying one thing after another.”* – Edward Bach
 
**Positive Qualities:**

- Balanced Strength
- Accepting Limits
- Knowing when to surrender

**Patterns of Imbalance:**

- Iron-willed
- Inflexible
- Over striving beyond one’s limits

- Aurum Met
- Nux Vomica
- Sepia
