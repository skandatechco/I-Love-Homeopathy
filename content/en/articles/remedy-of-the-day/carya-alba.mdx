REMEDY OF THE DAY: CARYA ALBA
Nogal americano, Shellbark, or Shagbark Hickory.
Tincture made of the nuts.
For Purpura, Scury
Antiescorbutic properties, haemorrhages from all surfaces, on the
least excoriation of skin very dark blood gushed out. Blood gushed
from gums, nose, ears and other parts.
Face clay coloured, swollen
gums begin to bleed and turn black, could not move lips without
causing profuse discharge.
Swollen abdomen.
Discharge of black bloody stool (melenas)
Short breath, difficult, quick pulse.
Limbs extremely swollen.
Body covered with livid spots from one incho to a pin's head size.
sometimes red like fliea bites.
Beatriz, MM John Henry Clake.
