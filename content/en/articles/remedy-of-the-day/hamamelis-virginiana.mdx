## REMEDY OF THE DAY: HAMAMELIS VIRGINIANA

Hamamelis has the soreness as if bruised in a marked degree.
When [Arnica](http://ilovehomoeopathy.com/arnica-montana-l-plant-healing/) fails hamamelis will work, rheumatism will do well,
Arnica acts in the capillaries, causin relaxation, Hamamelis acts
upon the veins, which are very full, enlarged and sore.
It is called the aconite of the veins.
