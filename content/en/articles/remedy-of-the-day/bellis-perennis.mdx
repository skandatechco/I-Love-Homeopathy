Tincture made of the whole plant when in flower.
			B-bruised - first remedy in injuries to the deeper tissues
			E-effects of cold or iced drinks when heated
			L-Lameness as if sprained
			L-Left sided medicine
			I-Injuries to nerves with intense soreness and intolerance to cold bathing
			S- stasis and fag are the key notes
			P-pelvic soreness with bruised feeling
			E-Exudation and swelling of all kinds.
			R-Remedy of old laborers especially gardeners.
Bruise indicates the main spheres of action of Bellis in injuries to the deep active tissues after major surgery: trauma of
			the pelvic organs.
