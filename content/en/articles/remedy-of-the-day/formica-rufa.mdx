REMEDY OF THE DAY: FORMICA RUFA
Red wood ant: AN ARTHRITIC, GOUT, ARTICULAR RHEUMATISM
Remedy with pains worse by motion and better from presure, warm,
rubbing and combing hair. Right side affected. Chronic gout and
stiffness in joints. Acute outbursts of gouty poisons, especially
when assuming the neuralgic forms.
Tuberculosis, CARCINOMA, lupus, CHRONIC NEPHRITIS,
complaings from overlifting weight. Has marked deterrent influence
on the formation of polipi.
Brain feels heavy and large. Senstion as if bubble burst in forehead.
Exhilarated, coryza and stopped-up nose, rheumatic iritis, nasal polipi.
Ret itching and burning, nettle rash, nodes around joints.
worse by cold, cold washing, dampness and before a snowstorm.
Beatriz HH july 8-7-11
