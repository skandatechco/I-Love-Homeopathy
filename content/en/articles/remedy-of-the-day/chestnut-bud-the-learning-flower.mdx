*"For those who do not take full advantage of observation and experience and who take a longer time than others to learn the lessons of daily life."*-Edward Bach
The theme of Chestnut Bud is *repeated mistakes.*
**Positive Qualities-**

- Mentally adept
- Enjoy learning
- Attentiveness & Observation
- Constructive learning from experiences
- Recognition of undesired behavioral patterns

**Imbalanced State-**

- Repeated mistakes
- Poor observation of life
- Failure to learn from experience
