Hurried Harry is restless and intolerably cross,
			Tendency to do some strange things without any cause!
			Constantly feels forgotten something, doesn't know what ;
			Sudden impulse to run and get violent,but motion: that really exhausts!!
			Thinks he's well,fear of physician as well,
			Melancholy with thoughts of suicide,usually he dwells!
			Being quiet makes him anxious,
			Glands are enlarged, feels cure is precarious!!!
