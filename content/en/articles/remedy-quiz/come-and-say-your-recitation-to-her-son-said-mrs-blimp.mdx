" Come and say your recitation, " To her son," said Mrs. Blimp
			And without much hesitation, "Haven't learned it !" cried the imp.
			" Isn't that just like our Willy, " He never plays n never learns"
			Said Mamma " He looks quite silly " When he gets these awful turns.
			" He always seems so cold n clammy, " I'm sure his chest's not very strong,
			" And if he gets a scratch" said Mammy
		" It festers and his skin goes wrong."
