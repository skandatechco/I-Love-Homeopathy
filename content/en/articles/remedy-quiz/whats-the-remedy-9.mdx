**Vigourless Victor has memory weak and mood indifferent,
Has marked photophobia which is so apparent :
Forces him to shut eyes, as light trembles when he opens,
Especially after sleep, this trembling worsens.
There's hypersensitivity to hearing which is unusual,
Can't take the constant humming sound as casual.
Poor Victor becomes senseless with colicky pain aroung navel,
Develops indigestion or diarrhoea with fever, as there's worms in his bowel.
Dislikes liquid food but lime juice he craves ;
Needs a remedy soon though canine hunger he braves !!**
