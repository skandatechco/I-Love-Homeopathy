Melancholic Mary is always in a hurry,
Weeps when talking and has a weak memory!
Fears that someone is behind her and is hopeless of recovery,
Alas! T'is makes her think that time is passing too slowly..
Has aching in eyeballs as if she's constantly staring,
Craves for liquor, though in pregnancy she has pernicious vomiting!
She's got a peculiar dream that she's drinking,
