Balbus Bibulus os Rome
			Who knew " Strong drink's a mocker,"
			Drank several bottles, toddled home
			And went clean off his rocker.
			He said " My friends I do declare
			" I think I'm Saint Augustin."
			He rent his clothes and tore his hair
			(His manners were disgustin').
			When Doctors Hic and Hoc were summoned
			He thought he would beguile 'em
			By saying he was Bulldog Drummond.
			Said Doctor Hic ' Asylum!"
