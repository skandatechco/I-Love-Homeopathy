REMEDY OF THE DAY!
ASCLEPIA TUBEROSA - The butterfly weed
Pleuresy-root. From the fresh root a tincture is made.
Its action is on the chest muscles
Sick headache with flatulence in stomach and bowels.
Dyspepsia, Bronchitis and peuresy.
Catarrhal states from cold and damp weather.
Irritation of larynx with huskiness, grip with pleuritic pain.
Catarrhal dysentery with rheumatic pains all over
Elevation of spirit > evening.
low spirit, fretfulness, weak memory
amorous at 9:00pm, but can turn irritable for nothing.
