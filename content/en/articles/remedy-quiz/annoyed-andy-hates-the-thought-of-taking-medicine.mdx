Annoyed Andy hates the thought of taking medicine,
			Is angered easily,takes a long time to forgive and take decision!
			His body is too large ,he has t'is impression,
			His sensitivity n pains,gets ameliorated with slight friction!
			Below the pectoral muscle, he suffers with thoracic pain,
			Feels better with hot water, but is worse with fresh air!
			Eyelids get struck in the morning on waking,
			There's yellow pus surely in the making.
			"My head", he says with pain as he's quarreling,
			A tight bandage with a right remedy,
			That's all he needs in the beginning !!!
