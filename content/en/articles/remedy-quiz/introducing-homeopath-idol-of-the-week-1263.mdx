IDOL: introducing Homeopath Idol of the week,
Dr. Sandra Chase, a pioneer Homeopath physician and advocate. She has been active in organizational Homeopathy since 1975, having donated much of her time regionally, nationally, and internationally. In 1995, she was elected the first woman President (1995-1998) of the Liga Medicorum Homeopathica Internationalis (LMHI), and the International Homeopathic Professional Medical Organization.
Dr. Chase is the author of a number of Homeopathic articles and pamphlets over the years, published in Homeopathy Today, and the American Journal of Homeopathic Medicine. Among many other awards, in February of 2005 Dr. Chase was selected as one of "America's Top Physicians", having been listed in the Guide to America's Top Physicians, 2004-2005 Edition.
Salutations to Dr. Chase for her remarkable devotion and dedications!
