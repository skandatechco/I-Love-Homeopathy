GRANATUM (Punica granatum)
Hungry Hazel has urging ineffectual, pain abdomen is usual umbilical.
gaining weight seems to be impossible, vomiting and vertigo is always palpable.
Feels the clothing harsh and oppressive, even sighing is weak and submissive;
Itching at anus is almost persistant,
Remedy is what she needs, though she is reticent!
Beatriz hh
