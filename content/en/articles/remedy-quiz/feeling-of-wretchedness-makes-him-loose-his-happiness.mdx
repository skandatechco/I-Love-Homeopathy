Feeling of wretchedness makes him loose his happiness,
Our unfortunate Andy becomes even more despondent.
Can't bear the incessant nausea and vomiting,
Even least motion increases this sickly feeling!
This deathly nausea gives him a vertigo,
Gosh!! Opening of eyes, increases it in a go!!
Though his hands and legs are icy cold,
His desire to uncover abdomen is pretty bold.
A suitable remedy awaits our Andy,
Surely he expects it to be homeopathically friendly !!
