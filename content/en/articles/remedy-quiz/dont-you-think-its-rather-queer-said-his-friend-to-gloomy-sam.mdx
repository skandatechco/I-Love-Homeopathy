" Don't you think its rather queer," Said his friend to Gloomy Sam.
			"First you vomit up your beer, " Then you eat that fatty ham."
			"You'll have to speak a little louder" To his friend, deaf Sammy said.
			The dandruff scattered round like powder
			As he scratched his itching head.
			His scalp was pustular and matted,
			It itched worse in a heated room:
			His face was red and super-fated,
		And even deeper was his gloom.
