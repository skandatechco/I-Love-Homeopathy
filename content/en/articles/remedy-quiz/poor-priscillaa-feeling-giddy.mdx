Poor Priscilla'a feeling giddy,
			Just as though she'd drunk " Red Biddy"
			Which she hasn't : poor wee kiddy,
			But she's feeling sick.
			Uncertain , coy and hard to please ;
			And when in bed she takes her ease,
			Her legs will twitch and kick.
			Her tummy's apt to ache and rumble,
			And yet she says she mustn't grumble,
			She's quite religious, meek and humble :
		She needs some medicine, quick !
