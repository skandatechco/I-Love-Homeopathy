Hardly feels inclined to frolic.
Her tongue is clean, no alcholic
Stimulants she's needing.

Are the symptoms she'll be getting
In that order, I'll be betting,
Along with bright red bleeding.
She fights for every breath she's taking
And feels that every bone is aching.
No slightest effort is she making
To get on with her feeding.
