REMEDY OF THE DAY;
CINERARIA MARITIMA , Senecio cineraria, Dusty Miller
Tincture of juice of the fresh plant, grows wild, and picked up
before the flower blooms.
Cures cataract and corneal opacities, juice diluted with water,
used externally installing into eye one drop four or five times a
day. This must be kept for several months, and it is more
effective in traumatic cases.
