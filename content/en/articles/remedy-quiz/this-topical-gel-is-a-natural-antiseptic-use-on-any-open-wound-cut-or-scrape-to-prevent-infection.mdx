Calendula gel (calendula officinalis): This topical gel is a natural antiseptic. Use on any open wound, cut or scrape to prevent infection. Promotes rapid healing of the skin. Soothing for other skin conditions such as rashes, cracked nipples, diaper rash. Will ease itching, stinging, red, chapped or irritated skin. For more serious wounds and burns, Calendula tincture, rather than the gel, can be applied to the dressing.
