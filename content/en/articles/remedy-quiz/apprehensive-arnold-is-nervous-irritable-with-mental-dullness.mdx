Apprehensive Arnold is nervous, irritable with mental dullness,
			Lack of air reduces pulse and gives giddiness.
			Even least exertion worsens the condition,
			As extreme breathlessness makes him tread with caution!
			The cutaneous chilliness and fatty degeneration,
			Gives blueness of fingers and toes with excessive perspiration.
		Being quite and at rest suits him the best..
