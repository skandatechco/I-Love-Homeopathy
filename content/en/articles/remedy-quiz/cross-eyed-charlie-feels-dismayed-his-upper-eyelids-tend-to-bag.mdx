Cross- eyed Charlie feels dismayed, his upper eyelids tend to bag,
			He hasn't been upon a jag, His favourite drink is lemonade,
			How can he expect to pick up, when he cannot eat his breakfast,
			He cannot even get an egg past His diaphragm without a hiccup,
			As onward to his club he toddles, He can't see straight,
			The pavement wheels in circles and his poor brain fails,
			As if inside his skull it wobbles...
