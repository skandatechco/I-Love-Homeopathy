Materia Medica : Ths is an important remedy in laryngeal affections. Also in scrofulous and tubercular affections of the glands. There is dry cough, with hoarseness and burning pain behind the sternum. Spasmodic cough, with rattling of mucus in the larynx; suffocative. Hoarseness. In diphtheria, where it has done some wonderful work, the membrane first forms in the bronchi, trachea or larynx and then runs upwards. From the chest (box)of medicines, which is this one, can you identify? Aparna Singh ILH 9th Aug 2012
