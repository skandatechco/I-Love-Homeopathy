Doctor, I am very nervous, restless. I get these pains that constantly move from one part of my body to another. I am so absent minded that I sometimes forget to collect the items I have just bought and walk away. I am finding very difficult at work, as a reporter I am unable to find the right words and use too many words. In my last article, I even forgot to finish it. I think this condition that I am in is hopeless and this condition is incurable. I get so angry that I fly off in a rage at the slightest provocation.

What's my remedy?
