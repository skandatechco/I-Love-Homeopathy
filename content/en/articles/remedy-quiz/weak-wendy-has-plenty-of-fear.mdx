Weak Wendy has plenty of fear
			Of falling : when standing and buzzing in ear ;
			Feels a band on head around,
			Makes it impossible to keep the hat on.
			Poor Wendy has burning when coughing,
			Makes the voice weak as pricking is increasing.
			Odour of urine is ever so peculiar
			Of violets it smells and doesn't seem to be clear.
			The weakness in limbs, of shoulder he feels,
			A remedy he needs to stop his head reel.
			
		Remedy Plss..
