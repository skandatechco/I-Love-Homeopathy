MATERIA MEDICA : The imbecility is more frequent than the active states of insanity or mania. An adult takes on the ways of a child, as a state of imbecility.This medicine also stands out in bold type for those prematurely senile; the man at fifty acts like an old broken-down man of eighty; he has lost all he had five or six years ago, and has taken on a child-like simplicity and innocence, an appearance of imbecility...There are many important medicines for premature senility and imbecility, and this remedy is one of these.. Can you spot this wonderful remedial agent ? Aparna Singh ILH 27th Oct
