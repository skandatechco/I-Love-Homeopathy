Still and listless,is he weak or careless?
			Weary Wrestly feels tired and sleepless.
			Feels in his eyes sand or sticks,
			Even has a feeling, his throat would split!
			His will power gets decreased,
			Especially when thinking is increased.
			Suffers from headache with fear:
			Of reading and writing,he always stands clear!
			Has peculiar sensation as if stairs are coming up to meet him,
			These delusions and weakness,can we really treat him? :)
