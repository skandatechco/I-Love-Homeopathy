Like Lach. this remedy has met with the most violent ..opposition from prejudice and ignorance, with its wonderful therapeutic powers have slowly, yet surely overcome. It was successfully used by Dioscorides, Pliny, and Sextus in ancient times, and revived in New York by Reisig, Bayard and Swan in the successful treatment of diphtheria and

Reisig was the first to potentize it. Diphtheria and Tonsillitis shining glaze appearance. For nervous restless highly sensitive organisms, erratic symptoms, pains flying from one part to another, changing from side to side.
			
Very forgetful Despondent, hopless, thinks her disease is incurable. Children cries and screams all the time. for people who have a hard attachment to the mother or did not have one and has a sense of abandon, babies that were not nursed by the mother.
			
Fear to be alone, fear of snakes, dying, becoming insane. Rage attacks Very hungry, menses too early. To dry up milk or to bring it back.
			
Sexual organs easily excited from touch, pressure and friction by walking.
