Weary Willie lies in bed ;
			His head feels small and full of lead.
			A lazy drowsy bilious fellow
			With saffron skin and motions yellow.
			His heavy limbs feel sore and quiver ;
			A dreadful pain assails his liver
			And, passing backwards, makes a raid
			Upon his right-hand shoulder blade.
			No wonder Willie stays in bed
			And cannot eat his daily bread.
