**CYNOPHOBIA** ( Fear of Dogs ) : ( Syco-syphilitic ) : An irrational fear of dogs ; It is an abnormal and intense fear of dogs . An abnormal and persistent fear of dogs ; Sufferers from cynophobia experience anxiety even though they realize that most dogs pose no threat .
Animal phobias are among the most common of the specific phobias and 36% of patients who seek treatment report being afraid of dogs or cats. Although snakes and spiders are more common animal phobias, **Cynophobia** is especially debilitating.
To avoid dogs , they may barricade yards or refuse to travel except in an enclosed vehicle .
Cynophobia is derived from the Greek word “kyon” means dog and phobos means fear .
