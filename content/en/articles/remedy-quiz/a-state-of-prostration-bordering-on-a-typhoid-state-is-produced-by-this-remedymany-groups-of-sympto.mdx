MATERIA MEDICA : A state of prostration bordering on a typhoid state is produced by this remedy.Many groups of symptoms are accompanied by cough, profuse glairy secretions.Coughs associated with catarrhs and affections of liver. "Sensation of coldness in the back, between the shoulders." It is generally found in chest affections, such as cough or pains in the chest without cough...Which is the remedy which deserves attention when these symptoms are present ?
