			Tommy tottered the tavern
			His throat was dry and ulcerated
			And felt just like a yawning cavern.
			All his glands seemed hard and tender ;
			he had a lump in either breast,
			But as he'd been upon a " Bender,"
			Still he muttered ,"that is Best ,"
			And as to bed with rising fever
			An aching limbs that quiver...
