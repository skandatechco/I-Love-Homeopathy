Fearful Fleming has pains all tearing,
			Coffee excites headache with a peculiar throbbing.
			Oh! This pain is from crown to eyebrows between,
			Its as if the bones were crushed alternating !
			Ouch! T'is pain in my wrist,
			Has swelling too on slight fatigue!
			Give me a medicine i'l show my gratitude,
			Even talking or eating gives me sudden lassitude!
