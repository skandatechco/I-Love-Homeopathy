			Smarting eyes and drooping lids,
			Tired, hot and very sweaty,
			Doesn't seem to love her kids.
			Her muscle tone is somewhat lacking
			All her pains seem bearing down
			And her cough is rather hacking ;
			She seems to wear a constant frown.
			Beth can't help it, please forgive her
			Though she spurns her kith and kin.
			Just imagine she's got liver
			Trouble -- and the cure begin.
