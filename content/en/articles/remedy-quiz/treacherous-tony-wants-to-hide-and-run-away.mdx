Treacherous Tony wants to hide and run away,
Frightened to talk loud which makes us feel diamay.
Thinks everyone is looking at her,
With tis unstability of mind, acts as if she is in a stupor !
Suffers from sick headache with blurred vision,
But the epistaxis or menstrual flow eases this neuralgia and tension.
Oh! her breath is suffocating which increases with rapid walking,
