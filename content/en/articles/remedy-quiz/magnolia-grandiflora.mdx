MAGNOLIA GRANDIFLORA
Stiff Stephe is quite with soreness, leaves him tired that's always doubtless;
distressed feelin like large bolus of food in stomach he feels, tha's ain't good!!
Is soffocated as he walks fast, has, "My heart stopped beating" he says aghast!!
T'is pain around heart , gave an itchy feet too, the erratic and shooting pains
are indeed very true.
Select a remedy which involves his rheumatism,
otherwise feel the pinch of Stephe's sarcasm....
