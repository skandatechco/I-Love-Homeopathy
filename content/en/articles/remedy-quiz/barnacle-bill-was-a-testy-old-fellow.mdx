Barnacle Bill was a testy old fellow
			Who didn't like fat,
			Though he wasn't quite as well as he seemed to be,
			He concealed it as he didn't like sympathy
			And he had a splitting headache but he didn't like to whine,
			And he frequently averred he had passion for the Brine.
			You could always pick out Billy from others on the ship
		By the fissured crack amidships which adorned his lower lip.
