REMEDY OF THE DAY
ASTERIAS RUBENS
Star-fish tincture prepared from the live animal, which is cut
in pieces and macerated in alcohol.
Histerical and neuralgic symptoms predominate, neuralgia left breast & arm
lassitud with red face, Mammae Cancer.
Sexual desire in both sex, uncommon cheerfulness in evening
Heat in head as if hot air surounding it
Constipation, stool like olives,
Beatrizhh
