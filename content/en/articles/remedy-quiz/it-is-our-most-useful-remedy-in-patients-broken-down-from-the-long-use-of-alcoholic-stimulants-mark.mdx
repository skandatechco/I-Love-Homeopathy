MATERIA MEDICA : It is our most useful remedy in patients broken down from the long use of alcoholic stimulants. Marked venous stasis in organs and parts. Worse from warm wraps, warm room, warm bed and warmth in general, yet he is sensitive to cold. EXTREME WEAKNESS in the morning; in old drunkards; in the heat of summer. Atrophy of the ovaries. Cancer of the uterus. Leucorrhoea acrid, bloody; burning; copious; before and after menses. This is a frequently indicated and long neglected remedy. Can the fans of Homoeopathy identify it ?
