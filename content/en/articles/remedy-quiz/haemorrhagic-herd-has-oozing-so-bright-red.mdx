Haemorrhagic herd has oozing so bright-red,
It's menorrhagia or lochia there's continuous shed;
Cannot take any motion on the least,
Though she's pregnant can't make herself please.
Even her bladder makes her condition worse,
T's painful micturition and persistant haemorrhage seems like a curse !
There's flow from nose not from uterus during her 'cycles'
A similimum is what surely she's entitled !!!
