REMEDY OF THE DAY
ACHILEA MILLEFOLIUM -Yarrow
Yarrow named Achillea by Linnaeus because the plant is mentioned
in the Iliad as having been used by Achiles, on the instruction of Chiron,
to heal the wounds of his soldiers.
Tincture made of whole plant.
Her action allied to its haemorrhagic power,
Good for pregnancy and the puerperal state, Varicose veins during
prengnancy. Leucorrhea in children
Great aversion to work, sensation as if forgotten something,
as if blood ascended to head.
Suited for the aged, atonia, woman and children.
Is suited for bleeding of uterus, wounds bleeding profusedly red bright . Milk abcense post partum
after surgery all glands, intestines, mouth, etc..
