Depressed Dell feels like hell
Says " Leave me alone, nothing to tell "
His sight is obscured, doesn't respond to light,
Has paralysis for sure, can't see his plight.
Walks backwards, with staggering gait,
Has profound stiffness, to tread he must wait.
Relaxed sphincter caused a surging,
Of lumps of mushy jelly stools,
Just thinking gave profuse urging,
Driving him back to gloom !
