A state of hyperesthesia in the acute affections, but the chronic affections are characterized by loss of sensation. Violent vomiting of everything eaten. Chronic gastric catarrh with vomiting of albuminous mucus and sweetish substance. With this symptom totality can you identify this wonderful remedial substance? Aparna Singh
