This remedy has particular action on the lymphatic system. Acts well in the peculiar disease called leucocytosis, which is when there is excessive white blood corpuscles in the blood. 

Also useful in indurated glands, especially Tonsils, breast cancer. Strumous Opthalmia, with tumefaction of cervical glans and stunted growth.
