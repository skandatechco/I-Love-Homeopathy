WHAT WOULD YOU PRESCRIBE FOR......JAMES TYLER KENT?
"I would rather be in a room with a dozen negroes slashing with razors
than in the hands of all ignorant prescriber of high potencies".
"A study of Dr Kent's life and achievement reveals three prominent attributes: rare intuition or perception, logical thought and a love of order so strong that implicit obedience to law and uncompromising devotion to principle were set as his ideal (W.W.Sherwood)... there was no room for comment on his ill-fitting and ill-assorted clothes .... Genial, gentle devoted friend to his patients and pupils; jealous guardian of homeopathy against the criticisms of those whom he considered his enemies; sensitive, embittered, retiring man in later years as he thought one after another did him wrong" (Julia Minerva Green, 1919)
