Depressed David dreads to be alone,
			Though he can speak.....that he's unable to understand is clearly shown...
			Hears someone talking and cannot bear the light,
			And when it starts to rain, he fears to see the sight !
			Loves to have buttermilk that is as sweet as can be,
		On swallowing, feels food turning cork-screw with acidity of marked degree.
