REMEDY OF THE DAY; CAPSICUM ANNUUM
The burning hot feeling, HOMESICKNESS with sleplessness,
red face, red chicks, fat, do not like to exercise or change
their routines. producing a sensation of constriction in all
mucous membranes, for elders with exhausted vitality,
myalgia with jerking and ached muscles.
Fears slightest draught, blear eye appearance who do not
react, disposition to suicide, wants to be let alone.
Has a general uncleanliness of body, peppery disposition .
Petrous bone inflamation. Fetid odor from mouth and lungs.
Throat dry and burning extending to ears, with sub-acute
inflamation of eustachian tube with great pain
ALCOHOLICS, Delirium tremens, abstainers from
accustomed alcoholics, postration and feeble digestion
of alcoholicas. Sore throat of smokers and drinkers.
MEN: Coldness of scrotum, with impotency, atrophied
testicles, gonorrhea with cordee, excessive burning.
WOMEN: Left ovarian sticking sensation.
COUGH; the peculiarity of this remedy is pain in
distant parts on coughing, bladder, legs, ears, sciatica,
Hahnemann mentions in his Materia Medica Pura that
is not suitable for persons with hard fiber muscles,
Herpes labialis (apply one drop of mother tincture)

thirst after stool with bleeding piles, stinging pain during
stool.

spurts. ectropion of meatus.
