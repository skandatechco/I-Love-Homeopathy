Grieving Greg feels so depressed
Cannot seem to move his chest.
When he sleeps his breathing stops,
Cheyne-Stokes respiration , has in his cough.
Lying down he feels suffocating,
Has Asthma with oppression and wheezing.
He wakes up with a start,
T'is disseminated mucous he cannot depart.
Has pain in his spleen which is so enlarged,
Chronic malaria seems to have embarked.
