Frowzy Fisher cannot remember,
For names he has to ponder.
Suffers from this mental depression,
Which is the root cause of his exhaustion ;
Meditating is like a curse,
Bathing in sea is still more worse.
Has watery stools and cramping pain,
Drinking water proved in vain !
Wishing for a miracle in the form of a remedy
Is there a cure for his common malady !
Remedy Pls..
