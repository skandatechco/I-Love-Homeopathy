MATERIA MEDICA : This remedy is useful when the mind is not insane and yet it is weak. Especially is it indicated in those persons who manifest a momentary, fleeting inquisitiveness, jumping from one subject to another, presence of other persons aggravates the symptoms; also the marked aggravation from conversation. Bloody urine with red sediment in the urine.(contd)
