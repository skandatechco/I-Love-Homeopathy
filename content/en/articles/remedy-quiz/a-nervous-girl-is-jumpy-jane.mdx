A nervous girl is Jumpy Jane
			Who's got one subject on the brain,
			Again she'll tell you and again
			The details of her every pain.
			A "cutting " pain assails her eye,
			A " shooting " pain goes down her thigh ;
			When on her back she tries to lie
			The " drawing " pain will make her cry.
			She gives in accents melancholic
			The details of her windy colic,
			And cries with wit that's almost Gallic
		" The centre of my pains Omphalic."
