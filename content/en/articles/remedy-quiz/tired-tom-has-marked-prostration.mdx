Tired Tom has marked prostration,
Becomes anxious and irritable without any causation.
Tongue is thickly furred and appetite is gone,
Suffers from hyperacidity and weakness is shown ;
Though it disturbs, has craving for water,
Eggs cause diarrhoea and whole digestion is shattered.
Palpitation gives a feeling his heart has stopped,
Open air relieves his suffocation but needs a pill to be popped !
