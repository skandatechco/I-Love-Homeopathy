IDOL: please meet the Homeopath Idol of the week,
Dr. Wendy Pollock, a Homeopath physician from Portland, who is practicing Homeopathy for more than 25 years; and recently has been volunteering for Health in Africa, a non-profit organization that treats AIDS patients in the Kilimanjaro region of northern Tanzania.
Dr. Pollock said she hopes to raise awareness about the effects of homeopathy on AIDS patients and teach local health practitioners to run sustainable practices.
"There is a huge need all over the world", she said, "this is one little tiny step, but it's an important one. My feeling is that if people could give a little bit of their time, we could solve enormous problems."
Salutations to Dr. Wendy Pollock, for her great dedications!
