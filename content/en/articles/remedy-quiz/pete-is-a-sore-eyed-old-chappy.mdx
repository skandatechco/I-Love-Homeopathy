Pete is a sore-eyed old chappy,
Who is frequently angry and rarely feels happy,
For he's troubled by such a peculiar thought -
He thinks that he's got a leg more than he ought,
And he'd like to go the Isle of Man,
But mal-de-mer interferes with his plan,
And he's giddy and squeamish and can't find his sea-legs,
In spite of the fact that he thinks he's got three legs.
So he stays at home and scratches his head
