MATERIA MEDICA : Running through this remedy a general state is a gouty or rheumatic state, in which the symptoms change with the changes of the weather; worse in cold, damp weather, worse from cold bathing, better from the heat of a hot bath. 
There are catarrhal symptoms in this remedy; catarrh of the nose, stomach, rectum, etc., and an increased flow of mucus from any of these localities from taking cold. He takes a cold bath at night on going to bed and gets up in the morning voiceless cannot speak a word. The skin is ulcerated and has a tendency to grow warts, callosities, bad nails and bad hair. What is this remedy we are talking about ? Aparna Singh
