Augustus was a skinny lad,
Peculiar symptoms Gussy had ;
He'd step out high like riding a bicycle,
And his chest was bad and probably phthisical,
And his limbs would twitch and quiver while waking,
And after a nap he'd find them all aching.
With pains in the bones,and what seemed more odd,
He would feel them on opposite sides of the body ;
And its hard for a fellow to feel nice and calm
With an aching right ankle and painful left arm !
If you have remedy for symptoms so precarious,
Atlast Augustus will be relieved without becoming serious !!
