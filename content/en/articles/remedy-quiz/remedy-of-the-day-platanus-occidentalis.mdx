REMEDY OF THE DAY, PLATANUS OCCIDENTALIS
Common name sycamore american tree, plane tree, buttonball tree.
Native of North America.
Tarsal tumors of lid, apply tinture both acute and old neglected cases
where destruction of tissue occurred and cicatricial contraction
caused marked deformity of lid, restored to practically normal
conditions. Acts better in children.
Must be used for some time, Ichthyosis
