"If anything annoys you give no heed to it;
if anything is too much for you have nothing to do with it;
if any one seeks to drive you go slowly and laugh at the fools
who wish to make you unhappy!
Be more prudent, consider yourself first,
let everything else be of only secondary importance for you."
~ Dr. Hahnemann
