Nervous Nandrea suffers from acute diarrhoea,
Along with colicky pain also has influenza.
On waking hates to have the food as nausea is so excessFlow of urine is more and after stool there's numbness Of lower limbs he feels; along with pain and heat.
With aching in bones and numb calves, can hardly stand on his feet.
Itching gives him weal due to the urticaria,
Is there a cure for his ongoing panacea!
