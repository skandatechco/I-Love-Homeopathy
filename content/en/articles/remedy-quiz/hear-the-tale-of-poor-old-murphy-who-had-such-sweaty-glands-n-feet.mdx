Hear the tale of poor old Murphy, who had such sweaty glands n feet.
			His itching nose was rather scurfy,And he liked odd things to eat.
			Fat and salt, he'd just adore them, Also herrings, sprats n chalk ;
			Bread n cake, he would abhor them, He said they made his innards talk.
			He'd wake up early in the morning, And couldn't get to sleep again,
		Feeling chilly, weary, yawning...
