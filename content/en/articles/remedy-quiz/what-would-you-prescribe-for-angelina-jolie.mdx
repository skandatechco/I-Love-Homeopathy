WHAT WOULD YOU PRESCRIBE FOR...... ANGELINA JOLIE?
"
I don't believe in guilt, I believe in living on
impulse as long as you never intentionally hurt another person, and
don't judge people in your life. I think you should live completely
free...
I don't see myself as beautiful, because I can see a lot of flaws...I like to hide behind the characters I play. Despite
the public perception, I am a very private person who has a hard time
with the fame thing...
I never like being touched, ever. People used to say I held my breath when they were hugging me. I still do...Honestly, I like everything, boyish girls, girlish boys, the heavy and the skinny. "
