Professor von Jaksh found a patient walking backwards,
Felt these reflexes exaggerated and awkward;
To laugh without reason,
and weep any season.,
Hemming all the time,
A low monotonous tone, is not a crime!
T'is chlorotic patient felt sore when touched,
"Ouch!" he exclaimed as he shrugged.
Slapping gait made his bones sensitive,
Prof. gave a Homoeopathic remedy, of course as a Curative !!!
