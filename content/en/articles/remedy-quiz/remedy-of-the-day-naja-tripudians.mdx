Simple hypertrophy of heart.
For restoring a heart damaged by acute inflammation, or from relief
of sufferings of chronic hypertrophy and valvular lesions.
Irritating, dry sympathetic cough in the acute stage of rheumatic
carditis, or chronic organic lesions.
Threatened paralysis of heart, post-diphtheritic.
