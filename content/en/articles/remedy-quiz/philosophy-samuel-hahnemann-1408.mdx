PHILOSOPHY- SAMUEL HAHNEMANN
In the first volume of the " Materia Medica Pura," published in 1811, nothing is said of the doses of the medicines. But in the latter volumes the doses of nearly every medicine are given.
In volume two, published in 1816, he recommends Causticum, a drop of the original preparation. Arsenicum is to be given in the twelfth, eighteenth or thirtieth dilution.
Ferrum, the 1/100, 1/10000, or 1/50000, of a grain is mentioned.
Ignatia is to be used in the ninth or twelfth potency.
Rheum, in acute affections in the ninth.
Hahnemann also later in the sixth edition says the more diluted or dinamized
will be better to have a soft, rapid cure without causing a severe aggravation.
