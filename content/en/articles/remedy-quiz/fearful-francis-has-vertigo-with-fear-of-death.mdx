Fearful Francis has vertigo with fear of death,
			Any exertion causes him to loose his breath !
			Feels as if his heart would stop,
			But rapid walking doesn't allow him to drop;
			Heartburn gives him profuse sweat and prostration,
			Though appetite is good there's always profuse salivation.
			T'is dyspnea and weakness he suffers throughout,
			Can we give a similimum to get rid of his bout !!
			
			Remedy Plss...
