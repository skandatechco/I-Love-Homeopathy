Every seventh year or so
			They vaccinated Scruffy Joe.
			He never took, which puzzled nurse ;
			His blinding headaches got much worse
			But still wouldn't let him be ;
			They dosed him up with cups of tea
			And then, according to reports,
			Poor Joe developed crops of warts ;
			His hair fell out, his skin looked dirty,
			And finally poor Joe got shirty.
			
		Remedy Pls..
