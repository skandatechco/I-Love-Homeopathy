Depressed Danny has problem with his memory,
Forgets happenings and people's names of which he was mastery;
Can't get a wink and dreams of quarrels he thinks,
Feels giddy at 10, in the morning with sensitive scalp , its when !
Rising from sitting, open air and stooping:
Makes his vertigo worse and gets his eyes drooping.
Tickling like a feather is buzzing in his ear,
Cannot open the mouth as there's always this fear!
