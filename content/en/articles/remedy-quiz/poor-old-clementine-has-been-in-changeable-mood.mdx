Poor old Clementine has been in changeable mood,
			Ever since she's lost her husband with whom she strongly stood!
			Smoking or smelling tobacco makes her headache worse,
			Desires indigestible things, to ordinary diet she's averse;
			Easily bites inside of cheeks, has small ulcers with swollen tonsils,
			Sexual frigidity worries her, a remedy is indeed a blessing!
