Pain in the head has Elizabeth Wise
And a curious habit of closing her eyes,
But with other folks sypmtoms she'll soon sympathise
Although she feels sad.
She explains her unsightly warts and acne and callouses,
Are not quite so painful as poor sister Alice's,
Who's at least twice as bad.
She's got gout in her knees and a chronic coryza,
We must surely device a cure for the ills of unlucky Eliza
