This is the tale of delirious Lotty, who staggers n mutters n seems rather dotty,
			And whose skin is dark red n has come out all spotty., Perhaps Scarlet Fever?
			Her throat's very sore n has started to swell
			The blood from her gums has a terrible smell
			Now they have phoned up to say she's unconcious as well--And replaced the receiver.
		Delay in the treatment is quite idiotic, For whatever the fever, it's clearly zymotic
