Even near fire Chilly Cherry feels coldness and prostration,
			Room and bed seems to spin, in vertigo has t'is sensation!
			Has nasal polyp with mouth distortion,
			Suffers salty belching with stringy offensive exudation.
			There's great tenderness with black persistent vomiting,
			Alas! on going to sleep, he stop's his breathing!
			Poor Cherry suffocating he awakens,
		Remove t'is fear and ailments, before his spirit dampens!
