This is the story of General Sisera
			Who after drinking had pains in his viscera
			And whose one sided headache was due to a nail
			Driven into his skull by a lady named Jael ;
			An action which some might consider indictable
			Although the poor fellow was rather excitable,
			And suffered from toothache and had nervous crises
			After divers - especially pleasant surprises.
