IDOL: please meet the Homeopath Idol of the week,
Jane Parkin, a dedicated Homeopath, who worked as a volunteer for the Maun Homeopathy Project, a charity in Botswana, for people who are HIV+.
She has more than 27 years of diverse clinical experience, ranging from a rural South African farm to busy private practices in London, U.K. and Cape Town, South Africa.
Jane has lectured in various components of Homeopathic education at the University of Westminster - London, and has taught practical homeopathic first aid classes internationally.
Salutations to Jane Parkin for her volunteer service and great efforts!
