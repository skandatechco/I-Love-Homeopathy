PHILOSOPHY - SAMUEL HAHNEMANN-ORGANON#63
Samuel Hahnemann, Organon Paragraph #63
Every agent that acts upon the vitality, every medicine, deranges more or less the vital force, and causes a certain alteration
in the health of the individual for a longer or a shorter period.
This is termed PRIMARY ACTION. Although a product of the
medicinal and vital powers conjointly, it is principally due to
the former power. To its action our vital force endeavors to
oppose its own energy, This resistent action is a property, is
indeed an automatic action of our life-preserving power,
which goes by the name of SECONDARY ACTION or
COUNTERACTION.
