IDOL: please meet the Homeopath Idol of the week,
Dr. Norbert Enders from Germany who is a volunteer Homeopath at VPHCS clinic in Sitio St. Mary Grace in Talamban, and in Sitio Lawis, Barangay Pasil, as part of his mission in the Philippines.
VPHCS (Visayas Primary Health Care Services) is a non-profit, non-government organization established in 1985 which aims to promote primary health care programs in the Visayas regions.
In this photo, Dr. Enders and Dr. Posadas (on the left) hold Homeopathy clinic in St. Mary Grace, Talamban, Cebu City on February of 2011.
Salutations to Dr. Enders and all staff of VPHCS for their great contributions!
