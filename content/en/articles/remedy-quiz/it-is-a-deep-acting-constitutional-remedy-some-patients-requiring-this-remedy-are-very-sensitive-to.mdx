MATERIA MEDICA: It is a deep-acting constitutional remedy. Some patients requiring this remedy are very sensitive to cold, and others to heat. Dropsy, external and internal. He is worse when hungry and, better after eating. Increasing loss of flesh and weight in pthisical patients; emaciation in children, extreme aggravation from slight physical exertion.The symptoms predominate on the right side. He suffers from a sudden impulse to kill somebody. Very talkative at times, mirthful; changeable moods and alternating conditions of mind; a degree of mental prostration prevails continuously.The voice is hoarse, rough and weak and finally lost; respiration is fast and asthmatic; respiration is difficult at night, on ascending, on exertion and motion, with palpitation; respiration is irregular, rattling, short, suffocative and wheezing.
		Identify the remedy. Aparna Singh
