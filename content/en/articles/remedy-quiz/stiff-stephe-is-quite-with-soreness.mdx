Stiff Stephe is quite with soreness,
Leaves him tired that's always doubtless ;
Distressed feeling like large bolus of food
In stomach he feels , that's ain't too good !
Is suffocated as he walks fast,
Has " My heart stopped beating" he says aghast !
T'is pain around heart, gave an itchy feet too,
The erractic and shooting pains are indeed very true.
