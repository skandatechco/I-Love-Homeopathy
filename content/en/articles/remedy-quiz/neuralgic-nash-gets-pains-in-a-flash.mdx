			Oh! Can't move even as least as a brush!
			Talking,sneezing or even biting gives pain like crushed with tongs,
			Occurs periodically at the same hour as if like a bang!
			Harsh hoarse voice sounds like a trumpet;"basso profundo"
			T's constant dribbling was enough to increase his complaints in a huge crescendo!
			Needs a remedy in a dash,
		For our poor Nash to celebrate his b'day bash!
