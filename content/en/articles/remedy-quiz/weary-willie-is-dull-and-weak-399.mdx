Weary Willie is dull and weak,
			His stomach's the cause,makes him bleak!
			Has diarrhoea with excessive thirst,
			Can't stop his vomiting,feels stomach would burst.
			With grapes ,pineapple,sorrel he can't resist,
			Though his coffee-ground stools still persist.
			Can we give him respite from his dyspepsia,
			So that he can enjoy his craving,without any panacea !!
