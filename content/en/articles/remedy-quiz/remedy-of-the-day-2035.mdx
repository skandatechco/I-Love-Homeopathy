REMEDY OF THE DAY
MEPHITIS PUTORIUS (skunk
Tincture made of the secretion obtained from the anal glands.
Experimented by Hering -chocking when eating or drinking.
This remedy is indicated in whooping cough when the paroxysms are so
violent that the child becomes blue and must be raised up in bed.
It also of service in false croup and laryngea spasms, when the child
feels suffocated and cannot exhale. Violent asthmatic paroxysms!
