Despondent David has a peculiar headache,
			Along with giddiness, feels head is rolling with the bed !
			Pain in the head increases and decreases with the fever,
			The yellow eyes with jaundice shows enlargement of spleen and liver.
			The hot, empty feeling at 11am, gives great desire to eat,
		Relishes cold things, but vomiting and eructations he cannot beat !
