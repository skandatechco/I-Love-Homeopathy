Haemorrhagic Hazel feels soreness so bruised,
Has albuminuria and neuralgia , but tries to remain poised.
There's affinity to buttermilk with dizziness on rising ,
Though haemorrhage is passive, she ain't compromising ;
Poor Hazel suffers from bleeding, stains indelible,
Can hardly recover from period, soreness is always palpable.
