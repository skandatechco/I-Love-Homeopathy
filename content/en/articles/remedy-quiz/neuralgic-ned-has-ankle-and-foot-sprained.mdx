Neuralgic Ned has ankle and foot sprained,
Pain in the eye ball left him fully drained.
Taking anything warm couldn't relieve his toothache,
Dysuria or wheez,walking aggravates.
The urge to urinate is hurriedly impelled,
For urine to appear, pressing he's compelled.
Beating of the heart becomes furious,
Even slightest motion is dubious!
A remedy is needed urgently,
That can make Ned move atleast gently.
Remedy Plss.
