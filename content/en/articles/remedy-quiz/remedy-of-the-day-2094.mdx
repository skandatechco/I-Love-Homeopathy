REMEDY OF THE DAY
CYCLAMEN EUROPAEUM
Tincture made of the root recollected in spring.
Ill humored-morose mood- he could easily take offence.
Extreme sadness, with weeping desire to be alone.
Internal grief and anxiety of conscience
as though he had not done nis duty- or had committed a crime
Diclination to work. desire for lemonade.
Flickerin dim vision, especiallly on waking, with spots before
vision of various colors.
Loss of appetite as he begins to eat.
Menses black, cloted and membranous.
chill and heat without thirst.
