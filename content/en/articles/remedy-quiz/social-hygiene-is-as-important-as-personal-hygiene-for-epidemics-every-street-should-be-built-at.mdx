"Social Hygiene is as important as Personal Hygiene for Epidemics - every street should be built at least 20 paces wide, so that the air could blow freely through it, and behind each house there should be a yard and a little garden; in this way the air could easily be renewed behind the houses.
In prisons, it is cruel to lock up several prisoners together, without allowing at least 500 cubic feet of air space to each. After the release of an inmate, a prison should be thoroughly cleaned and disinfected."
~ Dr. Hahnemann
