IDOL: please meet the Homeopath Idol of the week,
Dr. Nisanth Nambison, M.D (Gold Medalist) M.F.Hom (Mal), who is a researcher, teacher & clinician since 1998. Starting his career as clinician & researcher, he was offered post of Asst. Professor, Head of Surgery & Homeopathic Therapeutics, at the Government run Homoeopathic Medical College, Bhopal in India.
Dr. Nisanth Nambison has presented several research papers at national & international conferences. He has been appointed as Hon. Prof. Faculty of Homeopathy, Malaysia by her Highness Queen of Malaysia. A Post-graduate research scholar in Homeopathy, awarded with several National & International recognition including Bhartiya Chikitsak Ratna (New Delhi by the Minister of Human Resource Development Government of India), Star of Excellence 2000 (Malaysia), Fellow British institute of Homeopathy by the grandson Discoverer of Homeopathy (Singapore). He is also author of Pharmacokinship: Remedy Relationship.
Salutations to Dr. Nisanth Nambison, for his great contributions!
