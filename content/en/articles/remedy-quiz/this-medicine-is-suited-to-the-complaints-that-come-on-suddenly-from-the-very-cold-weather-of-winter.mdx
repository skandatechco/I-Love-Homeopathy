MATERIA MEDICA : This medicine is suited to the complaints that come on suddenly from the very cold weather of winter, or from the intensely hot weather of summer. It has the lung and brain complaints of winter, and the bowel inflammations and stomach disorders of summer. We know how these plethoric individuals become suddenly overheated and become violently sick. Can you recollect the medicine being referred to here ? Aparna Singh ILH 01 Dec 2011
