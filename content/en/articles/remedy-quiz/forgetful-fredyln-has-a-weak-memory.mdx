Forgetful Fredyln has a weak memory,
			Anticipates things almost correctly!
			Gets startled at small noises,though in a hurry,
			Stiffness of every joint leaves her in a quandary...
			The menstrual colic she feels intense,
			Must press feet against support,as in labor she pretends;
			Even dreams of drinking water,
			T'is constant thirst,will it get any better?
			Suffers from soreness of back and spine,
			Fredyln needs a simple remedy as gentle as mine!!!
