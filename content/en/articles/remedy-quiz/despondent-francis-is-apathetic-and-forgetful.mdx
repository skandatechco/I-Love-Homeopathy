			Hates salt meat as he always seems to be full.
			Oh! T'is retching makes the tongue so bitter,
			Even vomiting of green acid fluid doesn't make it any better;
			The lower right ribs is so tender to touch,
			Ouch! The pain becomes worse while walking,even moving as such!
