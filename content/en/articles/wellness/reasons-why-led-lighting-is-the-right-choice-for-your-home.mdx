The lighting in your residence plays an essential role in setting the tone and ambiance of your environment. These days, people can choose from more options than ever before when it comes to lighting their home.

**Bathroom Lighting**

Your bathroom is one of the places where you and your family spend a great deal of time. It's the place where you get ready for work, school, and the day ahead. For this reason, having a well-lit bathroom is critical to assure you look your best. Poor lighting can result in an uneven cosmetic application or not noticing small details about your appearance.

With multiple lighted mirrors options available, homeowners can select a style and size of the mirror that best suits their needs. Illuminated mirrors add the perfect amount of light to prepare for the day.

**Dress Mirrors**

Dress mirrors offer owners the convenience of having a large lit mirror to inspect their appearance before going out for the evening, for work, for school, or play.

With 400K natural daylight lighting, this type of mirror is ideal for applying cosmetics, as it produces a warm glow.

Table mirrors offer an excellent solution for anyone who prefers to have a smaller lighted mirror for use on their vanity. While smaller in size, they provide the proper level of lighting to assure that users look fantastic.

**Add Cool Sophistication To Your Home Or Office**

A lighted mirror is a sophisticated addition to your home or office. Not only does an illuminated mirror make your room appear more spacious, but it also offers gentle illumination. The LED lighting makes your surroundings feel warmer and more welcoming.

If you wish to extend the soft light beyond just your vanity mirror, installing LED lighting throughout your home contributes to higher levels of energy-efficiency in daily life.

For the most part, LED light bulbs are compatible with existing light fixtures, as they feature the same size and type of base that incandescent light bulbs use.

However, if you are using dimmer switches in your home, it's important to use LED lights that are compatible with traditional dimmer switches.

If the bulbs don't explicitly state they are compatible, they might not work. Another option to use a dimmer switch with LED lighting is to hook up an LED compatible dimmer to assure that everything operates correctly.

**Advantages Of LED Lighting**

There are numerous reasons to choose LED lighting solutions over other options that are available today. One of the largest benefits is that LED lighting is energy efficient. LED lights to use 75% less energy than regular incandescent light bulbs, in addition to lasting 25 times longer.

The LED lighting is functional and can get used in a variety of ways, including for outdoor lighting, under cabinet lighting, and in industrial and commercial applications.

Individuals who choose LED lighting for their home save money on their energy bills, and have to pay for replacement bulbs less often, making them the ideal choice.
