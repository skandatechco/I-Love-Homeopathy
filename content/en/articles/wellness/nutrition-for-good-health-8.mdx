NUTRITION FOR GOOD HEALTH
Polyphenols are a group of compounds which are present in all plant organs and are among the most popular and commonly consumed nutraceuticals.
There are various classes of polyphenols and one of them , called anthocyanins, impart color to the foods. These pigments impart deep colors such as blue, red, purple and black .

Health benefits offered by colored pigments include anti inflammatory effects, protection against cancer, bacterial infections, aging and neurological diseases, and diabetes.
