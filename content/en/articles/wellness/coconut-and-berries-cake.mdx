## Coconut and berries cake 🍓🌴GLUTEN FREE - DAIRY FREE - GRAIN FREE - 🍓🍓🍓

What you need: 
🌴150g coconut sugar 
🌴150g coconut oil 
🌴100g coconut flour 
🌴100g ground almonds 
🌴1 tsp of baking powder 
🌴1/4tsp bicarbonate of soda 
🌴3 eggs 
🌴zest of 1 orange 
🌴3tbsp orange juice 
🍓🍓🍓To decorate : coconut yogurt and plenty of berries
 👩🏻‍🍳What to do : preheat oven at 150c fan. Put all the ingredients in the food processor and blend until well mixed. Transfer into a cake tin ( greased) and bake for 45 minutes circa. When cooled decorate by spreading the coconut yogurt and adding the berries. A lovely gluten & dairy free treat for a special afternoon tea. This is part of your 20 folks
