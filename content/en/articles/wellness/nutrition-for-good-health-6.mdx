##### NUTRITION FOR GOOD HEALTH Magnesium is a macromineral that is present in bones and muscles, though not as abundantly as calcium and phosphorus. Magnesium deficiency has been suggested as a factor contributing to the pathogenesis of several chronic diseases. a decline in magnesium status can bring about anorexia, nausea, muscluar weakness, lethargy and weight loss.

Both dysrhythmias and myocardial ischemia have been attributed to low magnesium i[![](http://ilovehomoeopathy.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/417146_523955090949712_819210254_n.jpg "FOODS RICH IN MAGNESIUM")](http://ilovehomoeopathy.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/417146_523955090949712_819210254_n.jpg)ntakes.
Food sources include green leafy vegetables, shellfish, soya flour, tea, coffee, nuts and cocoa. Highly refined and polished cereals have extremely low magnesium levels.
