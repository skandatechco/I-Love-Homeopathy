### WHY BITTER FOODS ARE IMPORTANT FOR YOUR HEALTH.

##### 🍃Bitter foods moderate hunger and blood sugar

##### 🍃Because bitterness sometime represents toxicity this might be the reason why bitter foods reduce appetite

##### 🍃Eating a starter made with bitter foods is therefore a good way to start a meal

##### 🌱Bitter foods stimulate the liver to produce bile which is essential for the digestion of fats and the absorption of fat soluble vitamins such as A, E, D and K.

##### 🍃My favourite are radicchio, endive, rocket and kale but there are plenty more such as dandelion greens, bitter melon, Jerusalem artichokes, ginger and even dark chocolate ( 80% cacao) and coffee are considered bitter foods.

##### 🍃Adding a little bitterness to your meal not only increases its health benefits but also add an interesting new spectrum of tastes which we have forgotten in the current sweet-saturated culture.

##### 🍃Add some bitterness to your plate and keep your liver sweet ! 🍃
