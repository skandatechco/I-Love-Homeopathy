Baked apple with Oats recipe

&nbsp;

2 Apples cut topside and cored ..( keep the inside fruit aside,) 1/2 cup cooked oats ( add a pinch of salt) Raisins .. 2-3 teaspoons Almonds ( optional) chopped Honey 3 teaspoon Pinch Cinnamon Powder Core apple .. remove seeds .. cut the flesh into small pieces...                  Mix all ingredients together.. Apple, Oats, Honey, Raisins,

Mix all ingredients together.. Apple, Oats, Honey, Raisins, Almonds, Cinnamon powder .. Fill the apples with this mixture and bake in oven 180 deg for 15 minutes or until outer skin feels soft .. Remove from oven .. drizzle more honey ( if needed,) serve warm ... or serve cold ...
