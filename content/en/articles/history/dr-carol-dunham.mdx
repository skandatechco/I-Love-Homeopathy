HISTORY:
Please meet Dr. Carroll Dunham (1828-1877) a Homeopathic physician and dean of faculty at the New York Homeopathic Medical College. He was also president of the American Institute of Homeopathy.

In 1853, Carroll married Harriet E. Kellogg (1828-1878). They lived in Irvington of New York, and had six children. Dr. Dunham was an active Homeopath and conducted many lectures on Materia Medica.

Thank you Dr. Carroll Dunham for your great contributions!
