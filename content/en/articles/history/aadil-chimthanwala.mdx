Aadil Chimthanwala is a fourth generation Homeopath. He completed his medical degree with honours and then studied Homeopathy. His passion for cardiology led him to run a full-fledged homoeopathic hospital dedicated to cardiology, The Aadil Homoeo Heart Care Center, blending the art of Modern Medicine with Homoeopathic Philosophy &amp; Therapeutics.

He firm belief that knowledge should be shared inspired him to form The National Academy of Homoeopathy. He is the secretary of this organisation and in-charge of the Department of Clinical Medicine.

He is keen on provings and has proved over 18 drugs. An

Forth in the generation of Chimthanawala's who are in Homoeopathy since past 104 years, he did his MBBS with honours from Nagpur University and DNB (Internal Medicine). Then he has done BHMS graduation as well as MD Medicine in Homoeopathy from Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad, again with honours. He worked as the Resident Physician in the Department of Cardiology at the Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences and Super Speciality Hospital, Nagpur. He is the Secretary of The National Academy of Homoeopathy, India and is in charge of its Clinical &amp; Academic activities &amp; Projects. He heads the Department of Clinical Medicine of the Academy. He is looking after the overall administration and the conduction of the Post-Graduate Homoeopathic Residential Schools and the Late Dr J.N.Kanjilal Memorial Workshops. He is also the Project Incharge of the Statistical cum Clinical Research Project entitled ‘The Scope of Homoeopathy in Cardiological Disorders’. With his quick eye and clear observation, he has an uncanny ability to blend the art of Modern Medicine with Homoeopathic Philosophy &amp; Therapeutics. He has delivered lectures on Homoeopathy in Cardiology at National and International Seminars and Symposia like LIGA Medicorum Homoeopathica Internationalis at Redondo beach California USA on Tubercular Miasm and Cardiology in Homoeopathy and “Clinico-angiographic Profile of Young Indians suffering from IHD and scope of Homoeopathy” at the International Congress on Evidence-based Homoeopathy at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia and finally in the 66th LIGA Congress at New Delhi where he spoke on Cured Cases of Atherosclerotic Heart Disease. His contributions to the Academics of Homoeopathy range from publishing Books to being a regular contributor to various National and International Journals and Souvenirs. He is the recipient of “Dr. Hahnemann Award” for Excellence in Homoeopathy in 2010 and “Avantika Dhanwantri Award” in 2011.

He even conducted an International school in Australia recently.
He was invited as a speaker in an international Congress of Homoeopathy at Morocco in June 2015 and IHMA Chennai in August 2015.
He is also researching in Agro Homoeopathy along with his international students.
He is also the representative of The National Journal of Homoeopathy, Mumbai. He has 48 case studies and articles published in various peer-reviewed journals and he is the author of 4 books - Textbook of Miasms, Case taking and Management, Nosodes and Siddhi (an Exposition on Proved Drugs). He has also proved 18 drugs like Ferula Sumbul, Stannum met, Osteoarthritic nosode, Glonoine, Aconitum ferox, Ruta Grav, Spongia Tosta, Arnica Montana, Spigelia, etc.
