HISTORY: Please meet Dr. Edward Franklin, who was the physician of the Panama Railroad Hospital. In 1860 he was a teacher of Anatomy in the Homeopathic Medical College of Missouri. He was Professor of Surgery in the Homeopathic Medical College of the University of Michigan from 1878 to 1880, and Professor of Surgery and Clinical Surgery from 1880 to 1883. Dr. Edward Franklin was at one time president of the American Institute of Homeopathy, and was an honourary member of various state and national Homeopathic societies. He died in St. Louis, Missouri, December 10 of 1885.
