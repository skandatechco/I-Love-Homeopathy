HISTORY:
			The Ohio Homeopathic Hospital in 1914 with capacity of 35 beds, located at the corner of 10th and Neil Avenues, which belonged to the Ohio State University - College of Homeopathic Medicine. This was the first hospital on campus and was staffed by nurses and staff consisting of an assistant at night, a teacher of surgical nursing, a house physician, and eight student nurses. Ohio State rented a house on Neil Avenue across the street from the Hospital to provide a home for 14 graduate and student nurses.
