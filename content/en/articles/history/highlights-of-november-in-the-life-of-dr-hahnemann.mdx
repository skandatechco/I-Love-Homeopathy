History: highlights of NOVEMBER in the life of Dr. Hahnemann.
Nov 27 of 1748 Christian Gottfried, father of Dr. Hahnemann, married his first wife. She passed away nine months later after giving birth to twins.
Nov 2 of 1750 he married to Johanna Christiana Spiess, who became mother of Samuel Hahnemann.
Nov 17 of 1782 Samuel Hahnemann married Johanna Henriette Kuchler.
Nov 15 of 1784 his father Christian Gottfried passed away.
Nov 30 of 1786 the son of Samuel, Frederick Hahnemann was born.
