Dr. C. E. Sawyer (1860 ~ 1924), was a Homeopathic physician for the U.S. President Warren Harding.
Dr. Sawyer was a 1881 graduate of the Cleveland Homeopathic Hospital College (Cleveland, Ohio), earning his degree in homeopathy. He acted as the personal physician to Warren Harding and to Florence Harding as well. Dr. Sawyer diagnosed and successfully treated Mrs. Harding's kidney condition, the first doctor to do so, and thus gained her loyalty.
