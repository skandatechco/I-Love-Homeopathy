Please meet Dr. Samuel Lilienthal (1815-1891), a dedicated Homeopath physician from New York.

Dr. Lilienthal was the editor of the North American Journal of Homoeopathy, author of Homeopathic Therapeutics, and held professorships at New York Homeopathic College and New York College Hospital for Women. He also taught at the Hahnemann Medical College of the Pacific.

Thank you Dr. Samuel Lilienthal for your great contributions!
