HISTORY: please meet Dr. Bandhu Sahni (1925 ├óÔé¼ÔÇ£ 1997) a Homeopath Doctor and researcher with wonderful soul, from a small village called Karkauli in the district of Darbhanga in Bihar, India. He established a charitable Homoeopathic Dispensary for poor people. In recognition for his invaluable research many academic bodies and institution for Homoeopathic science has conferred upon him honorary degree. He was elected President of the Homeopathic Medical Association Of India, Bihar State Branch for two terms. Later on he was elevated to the National post of Vice President of The Homeopathic Medical Association of India. Dr. Sahni also served National Editorial Board of History of Indian Homeopathy.
