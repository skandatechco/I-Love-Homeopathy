BERBERIS is an excellent remedy for renal colic. The pains are of a shooting character. The patient cannot make the slightest motion and must sit bent over towards the painful side to obtain relief. If in addition, he complains of sharp, darting pains following the direction of the ureter and extending into the hip, there is no remedy like BERBERIS,
Dr. E. A. Farrington
