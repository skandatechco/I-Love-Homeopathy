## Homeopathy for Otitis

### Aconitum Napellus

In affections of the ear, Aconite is of service in acute otitis, with maddening pain. Here it is often superior to Pulsatilla or Chamomilla. General feverishness ; pain and soreness of the ear.
Temple S Hoyne
