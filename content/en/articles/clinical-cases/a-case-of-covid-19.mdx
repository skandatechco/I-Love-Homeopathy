Cough, Upper Respiratory Infection, Fever, Body ache and Fatigue.

Remedy: Veratrum viride 200CH - 4 times a day. Veratrum viride Q drops SOS if fever is more than 102f

Day 1: two doses - Fever persistent, but slept very well

Day 2: Remedies continued, Fever down to 99.4 by 4pm. No fever when going to sleep. Slept restfully

Day 3: No fever.
