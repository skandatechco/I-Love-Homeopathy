Miss XL, since seven years, **a short, abrupt cough from tickling in the throat,**
extending into the bronchi,
with sensation of concussion there ;
expectoration like saliva ;
aggravation at night ;
cough, waking several times during the night,
lasting about half an hour ; vomiting of food finished the paroxysm ;
vomiting also in day time.
Verat. 3, slight amelioration,
then **Phosphorus**, twice daily, followed by a rapid cure.
Dr. B. Hirschel.
