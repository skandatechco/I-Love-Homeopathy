After metrorrhagia over-sensitive, exalted nervous state ; hasty speech, accompanied by quick gestures; eyes fiery and glistening; pulse 110. Her 
movements were like those of a passionate person. If she could not talk, her friends had to relate stories continually in a quick way. At times she was in the opposite state, gave short answers and desired to be alone ; she was then moody and feigned sleep. The nights became more and more anxious and restless ; she could not describe how terrible they were.

What's her remedy?
