Here are some of the remedy resonance for Tonsillitis. Each of these remedies must be taken only when the totality of the symptom is indicated.
**Baryta carbonica**: The most prominent remedy for acute tonsillitis. Smarting pain when swallowing. Can swallow only liquids. Every cold settles in the tonsils and causes inflammation. Symptoms appearing on the right side.
**Fucus vesiculosus**: in chronic cases.
**Calcarea phosphorica**: In chronic enlargement of the tonsils in strumous children this remedy stands well in typical Calcarea cases. The tonsils are flabby, pale, there is a chronic follicular inflammation and impaired hearing.
**Calcarea iodata**: Enlargement of the tonsils similar to
Baryta. They are hard, red and nodular.
**Lycopodium**: Chronic enlargement of the tonsils, which are covered with small ulcers.
[**Ferrum phosphoricum**](https://www.bahola.co/product/ferrum-phosphoricum/?v=c86ee0d9d7ed): Chronic enlarged hyperaemic tonsils ; smooth swelling.
**Belladonna**: Tonsils are enlarged with redness. Burning, dryness and a sense of constriction. Symptoms on the right sided
**Amygdala Persica**: has dark injection of the fauces, sharp pains, and difficult swallowing.
**Phytolacca**: Especially indicated in the follicular form, with pain at the root of the tongue or extending to the ears when swallowing. The parts are dark blue ; the tonsils are large and blue, and there is intense dryness, smarting and burning in the throat
**Ignatia**: It is almost specific in follicular tonsillitis. Small superficial yellowish white ulcers ; plug in throat, worse when not swallowing.
**Guaiacum**: One of the most useful remedies at the commencement of an attack, especially of catarrhal tonsillitis.
The characteristics are violent burning, headache, throat hot>
chill and aching in back and limbs ; abscesses form quickly.
**Hepar sulphur**: Where there are lancinating pains>
